Jesus Bids Us Shine

Verse 1:
Je-sus bids us shine with a pure, clear light,
Like a lit-tle can-dle burn-ing in the night.
In this world of dark-ness so let us shine,
You in your small cor-ner, and I in mine.

Verse 2:
Je-sus bids us shine, first of all for Him;
Well He sees and knows it, if our light grows dim.
He looks down from Heav-en to see us shine,
You in your small cor-ner, and I in mine.

Verse 3:
Je-sus bids us shine, then, for all a-round;
Man-y kinds of dark-ness in the world are found:
Sin and want and sor-row; so we must shine,
You in your small cor-ner, and I in mine.

Verse 4:
Je-sus bids us shine, as we work for Him,
Bring-ing those that wan-der from the paths of sin;
He will ev-er help us, if we_ shine,
You in your small cor-ner, and I in mine.


---
Author: Susan B. Warner, 1868
