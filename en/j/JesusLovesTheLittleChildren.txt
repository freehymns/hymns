Jesus Loves The Little Children

Verse 1:
Je-sus loves the chil-dren dear,
Them He blessed when He was here,
And He nev-er turned the lit-tle ones a-way;
But “For-bid them not,” said He;
“Let the chil-dren come to Me,”
For as Sav-ior He’s the Life, the Truth, the Way.

Chorus:
Je-sus loves the lit-tle chil-dren,
All the chil-dren of the world,
Ev-’ry coun-try, ev-’ry race,
Loves them all from an-y place;
Je-sus loves the lit-tle chil-dren of the world.

Verse 2:
Twas for them that Je-sus died,
On the cross was cru-ci-fied,
Shed His blood to wash their man-y sins a-way;
Now who-ev-er will be-lieve,
Life e-ter-nal shall re-ceive,
And He’ll keep them in His care from day to day.

Verse 3:
Man-y thou-sands die each day,
In the coun-tries far a-way,
Who have nev-er heard of Je-sus and His love;
Let us pray, and give, and go,
That these lit-tle ones may know
Of sal-va-tion and the joys of heav’n a-bove.

Verse 4:
Let us tell the sto-ry o’er
Till it reach-es ev-’ry shore,
And the Gos-pel has been heard in ev-’ry land;
Till in ev-’ry tribe and tongue
Our Re-deem-er’s praise is sung,
Oh, the great re-demp-tion cho-rus ‘twill be grand!


---
Author: Edwin L. Bowyer, 1921
Author, Chorus: C. Herbert Woolston (1856–1927), alt.
