Jesus! What A Friend For Sinners

Verse 1:
Je-sus! what a friend for sin-ners!
Je-sus! Lov-er of my soul;
Friends may fail me, foes as-sail me,
He, my Sav-ior, makes me whole.

Refrain:
Hal-le-lu-jah! what a Sav-ior!
Hal-le-lu-jah! what a friend!
Sav-ing, help-ing, keep-ing, lov-ing,
He is with me to the end.

Verse 2:
Je-sus! what a strength in weak-ness!
Let me hide my-self in Him.
Tempt-ed, tried, and some-times fail-ing,
He, my strength, my vic-t'ry wins.

Verse 3:
Je-sus! what a help in sor-row!
While the bil-lows o'er me roll,
E-ven when my heart is break-ing,
He, my com-fort, helps my soul.

Verse 4:
Je-sus! what a guide and keep-er!
While the tem-pest still is high,
Storms a-bout me, night o-ver-takes me,
He, my pi-lot, hears my cry.

Verse 5:
Je-sus! I do now re-ceive Him,
More than all in Him I find.
He hath grant-ed me for-give-ness,
I am His, and He is mine.


---
Author: J. Wilbur Chapman, 1910
Tune: Hyfrydol
