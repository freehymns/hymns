Jesus Our Savior

Verse 1:
Je-sus our Sav-ior and our Lord!
To Thee we lift our eyes;
Teach and in-struct us by Thy word,
And make us tru-ly wise.

Verse 2:
Make us to know and un-der-stand
Thy whole re-veal-èd will;
Fain would we learn to com-pre-hend
Thy love more clear-ly still.

Verse 3:
Lord! May Thy word our thoughts en-gage
In each per-plex-ing case;
Help us to feed on ev-'ry page,
And grow in ev-'ry grace.

Verse 4:
Oh let it pu-ri-fy our heart,
And guide us all our days!
Thy won-ders, Lord, to us im-part,
And Thou shalt have the praise!

Author: Hugh Stowell (1799-1865)
