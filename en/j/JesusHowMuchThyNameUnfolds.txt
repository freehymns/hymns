Jesus! How Much Thy Name Unfolds

Verse 1:
Je-sus! how much Thy name un-folds
To ev-'ry o-pened ear;
The par-doned sin-ner’s mem-'ry holds
None oth-er half so dear.

Verse 2:
Thy name en-cir-cles ev-'ry grace
That God as man could show;
There on-ly could He ful-ly trace
A life di-vine be-low.

Verse 3:
Je-sus, it speaks a life of love,
Of sor-rows meek-ly borne;
It tells of sym-pa-thy a-bove
What-ev-er makes us mourn.

Verse 4:
Je-sus, the One who knew no sin,
Made sin to make us just;
Thou gav’st Thy-self our love to win —
Our full con-fid-ing trust.

Verse 5:
The men-tion of Thy name shall bow
Our hearts to wor-ship Thee;
The chief-est of ten thou-sand Thou,
Whose love has set us free.

Author: Mary Bowley Peters (1813-1856)
