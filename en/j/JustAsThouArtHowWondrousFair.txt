Just As Thou Art, How Wondrous Fair

Verse 1:
Just as Thou art, how won-drous fair,
Lord Je-sus, all Thy mem-bers are!
A life di-vine to them is giv'n,
A long in-her-it-ance in heav'n.

Verse 2:
Just as I was, I came to Thee,
An heir of wrath and mi-ser-y;
Just as Thou art be-fore the throne,
I stand in right-eous-ness: Thine own.

Verse 3:
Just as Thou art, how won-drous free:
Loosed by the sor-rows of the tree:
Sav-ior! the curse, the wrath were Thine,
To give Thy saints this life di-vine.

Verse 4:
Just as Thou art, O bliss-ful ray,
That turned my dark-ness in-to day!
That woke me from my death in sin
To know my per-fect-ness in Him.

Verse 5:
Soon, soon, 'mid joys on joys un-told,
Thou wilt this grace and love un-fold,
Till worlds on worlds a-dor-ing see
The part Thy mem-bers have in Thee.


---
Author: J. Denham Smith (1817-1889)
Tune: Ernan
