Jesus Christ, Thou King Of Glory

Verse 1:
Je-sus Christ, Thou King of glo-ry!
Born a Sav-ior-Prince to be,
While the an-gel-hosts a-dore Thee,
We joy in Thee!
Sing-ing of Thy grace the sto-ry,
Praise, praise to Thee!

Verse 2:
Thou the bands of death didst sev-er,
Con-flict Thine and vic-to-ry!
God is for us now and ev-er!
We joy in Thee!
We are Thine, Thine own for ev-er,
Praise, praise to Thee!

Verse 3:
Thou the ran-som price hast giv-en,
Set-ting thus the cap-tive free!
Thou art Lord of earth and heav-en,
We joy in Thee!
Through Thy blood we stand for-giv-en,
Praise, praise to Thee!

Verse 4:
Ris-en Lord! At Thy re-turn-ing
Sweet and full our song shall be;
Hast-ing to that bliss-ful morn-ing,
We joy in Thee!
Thou hast read our spir-its' yearn-ing!
Praise, praise to Thee!


---
Author: Hannah K. Burlingham (1842-1901)
Tune: StarOfPeace
