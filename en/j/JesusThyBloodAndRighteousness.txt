Jesus, Thy Blood And Righteousness

Verse 1:
Je-sus, Thy blood and right-eous-ness,
My beau-ty are, my glor-ious dress;
Midst flam-ing worlds, in these ar-rayed,
With joy shall I lift up my head.

Verse 2:
The ho-ly, meek, un-spot-ted Lamb,
Who from the Fa-ther's bos-om came,
Who died, my sins thus to a-tone,
And now my Lord and God I own.

Verse 3:
Lord, I be-lieve Thy pre-cious blood,
Which at the mer-cy seat of God,
For-ev-er doth for sin-ners plead,
For me, e'en for my soul was shed.

Verse 4:
When from the dust of death I rise
To claim my man-sion in the skies,
E’en then this shall be all my plea,
Je-sus hath lived, hath died for me.

Verse 5:
Je-sus, be end-less praise to Thee,
Whose bound-less mer-cy hath for me
And all who trust Thy hands hath made
An ev-er-last-ing ran-som paid.

Verse 6:
Ah! give to all, al-might-y Lord,
The pow’r to speak Thy gra-cious word,
That all who to Thy wounds will flee
May find e-ter-nal life in Thee.

Verse 7:
Oh, let the dead now hear Thy voice!
Now bid Thy ban-ished ones re-joice;
Their beau-ty this, their glor-ious dress,
Je-sus, Thy blood and right-eous-ness.


---
Author: Nicolaus Ludwig Graf von Zinzendorf, 1739
Original Language: German
Translator: John Wesley (1703-1791)
Tune: Germany
