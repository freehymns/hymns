Jesus, Wondrous Savior!

Verse 1:
Je-sus, won-drous Sav-ior!
Christ, of kings the king!
An-gels fall be-fore Thee,
Pros-trate wor-ship-ing;
Fair-est they con-fess Thee
In the Heav’n a-bove.
We would sing Thee fair-est
Here in hymns of love.

Verse 2:
Fair-er far than sun-light
Un-to eyes that wake
A-mid fear and dark-ness,
Til the morn-ing break;
Fair-er than the day-dawn,
Hills and dal-es along,
When its tide of glo-ry
Wakes the tide of song.

Verse 3:
Sweet-er far than mu-sic
Quiv-er-ing from keys
That un-bind all feel-ing
With strange har-mo-nies.
Thou art more and dear-er
Than all min-strel-sy;
On-ly in Thy pres-ence
Can joy’s full-ness be.

Verse 4:
All earth’s flow-ing pleas-ures
Were a win-try sea,
Heav’n it-self with-out Thee
Dark as night would be.
Lamb of God! Thy glo-ry
Is the light a-bove.
Lamb of God! Thy glo-ry
Is Thy life of love.

Verse 5:
Life is death if sev-ered
From Thy throb-bing heart.
Death with life a-bun-dant
At Thy touch would start.
Worlds and men and an-gels
All con-sist in Thee:
Yet Thou cam-est to us
In hu-mil-i-ty.

Verse 6:
Je-sus! All per-fec-tions
Rise and end in Thee;
Bright-ness of God’s glo-ry
Thou, e-ter-nal-ly,
Fa-vored be-yond meas-ure
They Thy face who see;
May we gra-cious Sav-ior,
Share this ec-sta-sy.


---
Author: Daniel A. McGregor, 1889 
Tune: Franconia
