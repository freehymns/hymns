Jesus, Thou Alone Art Worthy

Verse 1:
Je-sus, Thou a-lone art wor-thy
Cease-less prais-es to re-ceive;
For Thy love and grace and good-ness
Rise o'er all our thoughts con-ceive.

Verse 2:
With a-dor-ing hearts, we rend-er
Hon-or to Thy pre-cious Name,
O-ver-flow-ing with Thy mer-cies,
Far and wide Thy worth pro-claim.

Verse 3:
Praise Him! praise Him! praise the Sav-ior!
Saints, a-loud your voic-es raise;
Praise Him! praise Him! 'till in heav-en
Per-fect-ed we'll sing His praise.


---
Author: Mrs. J. A. Trench
Tune: Stuttgart
