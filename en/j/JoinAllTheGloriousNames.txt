Join All the Glorious Names

Verse 1:
Join all the glor-ious names
Of wis-dom, love and pow'r
That mor-tals ev-er knew,
That an-gels ev-er bore;
All are too mean to speak His worth,
Too mean to set the Sav-ior forth.

Verse 2:
Great Proph-et of our God!
Our tongues must bless Thy name,
By whom the joy-ful news
Of free sal-va-tion came;
The joy-ful news of sins for-giv'n,
Of hell sub-dued, of peace with heav'n.

Verse 3:
Thou art our Coun-sel-or,
Our Pat-tern and our Guide,
And Thou our Shep-herd art;
Oh, keep us near Thy side;
Nor let our feet e'er turn a-stray,
To wan-der in the crook-ed way.

Verse 4:
We love the Shep-herd's voice;
His watch-ful eyes shall keep
Our pil-grim-souls a-mong
The thou-sands of God's sheep;
He feeds His flock, He calls their names,
And gen-tly leads the tend-er lambs.

---
Author: Isaac Watts
Tune: Darwalls148th
