Jesus, In His Heavenly Glory

Verse 1:
Je-sus, in His heav'n-ly glo-ry,
Sits with God up-on the throne;
Now no more to be for-sak-en,
His hu-mil-i-a-tion gone.

Verse 2:
Nev-er more shall God, Je-ho-vah,
Smite the Shep-herd with the sword;
Ne'er a-gain shall cru-el sin-ners,
Set at nought our glor-ious Lord.

Verse 3:
Dwell-ing in e-ter-nal sun-shine
Of the coun-te-nance of God,
Je-sus fills all heav'n with in-cense
Of His rec-on-cil-ing blood.

Verse 4:
On His heart our nam-es are grav-en,
On His shoul-ders we are borne;
Of our God be-loved in Je-sus,
We can love Him in re-turn.


---
Author: Robert C. Chapman (1803-1902)
Tune: Stuttgart
