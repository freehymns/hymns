Whom Have We, Lord, But Thee

Verse 1:
Whom have we, Lord, but Thee,
Soul thirst to sat-is-fy?
Ex-haust-less spring! The wa-ters free!
All oth-er streams are dry.

Verse 2:
Our hearts by Thee are set
On bright-er things a-bove;
Strange that we ev-er should for-get
Thine own most faith-ful love.

Verse 3:
Yet oft we cred-it not
That Thou dost give as God,
Though well we know our hap-py lot
In trust-ing to Thy blood.

Verse 4:
None like the ran-somed host
That pre-cious blood have known;
Re-demp-tion gives faith's ho-ly boast
To draw so near the throne.

Verse 5:
High-er and high-er yet!
Plead-ing that same life-blood,
We rise as-sured our need is met
In Thee and Ab-ba God.


---
Author: Mary Bowley Peters (1813-1856)
Tune: Trentham


---Original
We rise as-sured our need is met=We taste the love that knows no let,
In Thee and Ab-ba God=Of Ab-ba, as of God
