With Jesus In Our Midst

Verse 1:
With Je-sus in our midst
We gath-er round the board;
Though man-y, we are one in Christ,
One bod-y in the Lord.

Verse 2:
Our sins were laid on him
When bruised on Cal-va-ry;
For us he died, and rose a-gain,
A pledge of vic-to-ry.

Verse 3:
Faith eats the bread of life,
And drinks the liv-ing wine;
Thus we, in love to-geth-er knit,
On Je-sus' breast re-cline.

Verse 4:
Soon shall the night be gone,
And we with Je-sus reign;
The mar-riage sup-per of the Lamb
Shall ban-ish all our pain.


---
Author: Robert C. Chapman (1803-1902)
Tune: Dennis
