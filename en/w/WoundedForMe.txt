Wounded For Me, Wounded For Me

Verse 1:
Wound-ed for me, wound-ed for me,
There on the cross He was wound-ed for me;
Gone my trans-gres-sions, and now I am free,
All be-cause Je-sus was wound-ed for me.

Verse 2:
Dy-ing for me, dy-ing for me,
There on the cross He was dy-ing for me;
Now in His death my re-demp-tion I see,
All be-cause Je-sus was dy-ing for me.

Verse 3:
Ris-en for me, ris-en for me,
Up from the grave He has ris-en for me;
Now ev-er-more from death’s sting I am free,
All be-cause Je-sus has ris-en for me.

Verse 4:
Liv-ing for me, liv-ing for me,
Up in the skies He is liv-ing for me;
Dai-ly He’s plead-ing and pray-ing for me,
All be-cause Je-sus is liv-ing for me.

Verse 5:
Com-ing for me, com-ing for me,
One day to earth He is com-ing for me;
Then with what joy His dear face I shall see,
O how I praise Him, He’s com-ing for me!


---
Author: William G. J. Ovens (1869–1945)
Author, verses 2-5: Gladys W. Roberts (1888–1983)
