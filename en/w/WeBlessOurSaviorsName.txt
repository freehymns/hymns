We Bless Our Savior'S Name

Verse 1:
We bless our Sav-ior's name,
Our sins are all for-giv'n
To suf-fer, once to earth He came,
He now is crowned in Heav'n.

Verse 2:
His pre-cious blood was shed,
His bod-y bruised for sin;
Re-mem-bring this, we take the bread,
And joy-ful drink the wine.

Verse 3:
While we re-mem-ber Thee,
Lord, in our midst ap-pear!
Let each by faith Thy bod-y see,
While we as-sem-ble here.

Verse 4:
Lord, let us ne'er for-get,
Thy rich, Thy pre-cious love;
Our theme of joy and won-der here,
Our end-less song a-bove!

Verse 5:
Lord, let Thy love con-strain
Our souls to cleave to Thee!
And ev-er in our hearts re-main
That word: "Re-mem-ber Me!"


---
Author: James G. Deck (1807-1884)
Tune: Silchester
