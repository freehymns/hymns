What Was It, Blessèd God

Verse 1:
What was it, bless-èd God,
Led Thee to give Thy Son,
To yield Thy well-be-loved
For us by sin un-done?
’Twas love un-bound-ed led Thee thus
To give Thy well-be-loved for us.
To give Thy well-be-loved for us.

Verse 2:
What led Thy Son, O God,
To leave Thy throne on high,
To shed His pre-cious blood,
To suf-fer and to die?
’Twas love, un-bound-ed love to us,
Led Him to die and suf-fer thus.
Led Him to die and suf-fer thus.

Verse 3:
What moved Thee to im-part
Thy Spir-it from a-bove,
There-with to fill our heart
With heav'n-ly peace and love?
’Twas love, un-bound-ed love to us,
Moved Thee to give Thy Spir-it thus.
Moved Thee to give Thy Spir-it thus.

Verse 4:
What love to Thee we owe,
Our God, for all Thy grace!
Our hearts may well o’er-flow
In ev-er-last-ing praise:
Make us, O God, to praise Thee thus
For all Thy bound-less love to us.
For all Thy bound-less love to us.


---
Author: Ann T. Gilbert (1782–1866)
Tune: Lenox

Original
Help us=Make us
