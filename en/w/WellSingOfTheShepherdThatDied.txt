We'll Sing Of The Shepherd That Died

Verse 1:
We'll sing of the Shep-herd that died,
That died for the sake of the flock,
His love to the ut-most was tried,
But firm-ly en-dured as a rock.

Verse 2:
When blood from a vic-tim must flow,
This Shep-herd by pit-y was led
To stand be-tween us and the foe,
And will-ing-ly died in our stead.

Verse 3:
Our song, then, for-ev-er shall be
Of the Shep-herd who gave Him-self thus;
No sub-ject's so glor-ious as He,
No theme so af-fect-ing to us.

Verse 4:
Of Him and His love we will sing,
His prais-es our tongues shall em-ploy,
Till heav-en-ly an-thems we bring
In yon-der bright re-gions of joy.


---
Author: Thomas Kelly (1769-1854)
Tune: Celeste
