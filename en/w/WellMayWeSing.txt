Well May We Sing, With Triumph Sing

Verse 1:
Well may we sing, with tri-umph sing,
The great Re-deem-er's praise!
The glo-ries of the liv-ing God,
Re-vealed in Je-sus' face.

Verse 2:
The love of God it was that sought
From sin to set us free,
That gave the Son, whose pre-cious blood
Has wrought our lib-er-ty.

Verse 3:
In Him we read the Fa-ther's love,
And find e-ter-nal peace;
We meet in Him a Sav-ior-God,
And fear and ter-ror cease.

Verse 4:
Then glad-ly sing, and sound a-broad
The great Re-deem-er's praise;
The glo-ries of the liv-ing God,
The rich-es of His grace!


---
Author: G. V. Wigram (1805-1879)
Tune: Azmon
