Wonderful Story of Love

Verse 1:
Won-der-ful sto-ry of love;
Tell it to me a-gain;
Won-der-ful sto-ry of love;
Wake the im-mor-tal strain!
An-gels with rap-ture an-nounce it,
Shep-herds with won-der re-ceive it;
Sin-ner, O won’t you be-lieve it?
Won-der-ful sto-ry of love,

Refrain:
Won-der-ful! Won-der-ful!
Won-der-ful, won-der-ful sto-ry of love.

Verse 2:
Won-der-ful sto-ry of love;
Though you are far a-way;
Won-der-ful sto-ry of love;
Still He doth call to-day;
Call-ing from Cal-va-ry’s moun-tain,
Down from the crys-tal bright foun-tain,
E’en from the dawn of cre-a-tion,
Won-der-ful sto-ry of love,

Verse 3:
Won-der-ful sto-ry of love;
Je-sus pro-vides a rest;
Won-der-ful sto-ry of love;
For all the pure and blest,
Rest in those man-sions a-bove us,
With those who’ve gone on be-fore us,
Sing-ing the rap-tur-ous cho-rus,
Won-der-ful sto-ry of love,

Author: John Merritte Driver, 1892
