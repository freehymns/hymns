What Rich Eternal Bursts Of Praise

Verse 1:
What rich e-ter-nal bursts of praise
Shall fill yon courts thro' end-less days,
When time shall cease to be!
A-round that throne the notes shall swell,
As each re-deemed one joins to tell
Thy love, so vast and free!

Verse 2:
Each shall the Sav-ior's like-ness bear,
A roy-al crown each brow shall wear,
With robes un-sul-lied white,
The ev-er-last-ing song shall be,
To Thee, O Lamb of God, to Thee,
'Mid scenes of pur-est light.

Verse 3:
Our joy un-hin-dered then with Thee,
Our eyes un-dimmed Thy glo-ry see,
Whilst wor-thy praise we give,
Through that e-ter-nal cloud-less day,
Our burn-ing hearts with rap-ture say,
He died that we might live.


---
Author: G. W. Frazer (1830-1896)
Tune: Piety
