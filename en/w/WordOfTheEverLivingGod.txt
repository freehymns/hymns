Word of the Ever Living God

Verse 1:
Word of the ev-er liv-ing God!
Will of His glor-ious Son!
With-out Thee how could earth be trod,
Or heav'n it-self be won?

Verse 2:
Yet, to un-fold Thy hid-den worth,
Thy mys-t'ries to re-veal,
That Spir-it which first gave Thee forth
Thy vol-ume must un-seal!

Verse 3:
And we, if we a-right would learn
The wis-dom it im-parts,
Must to its heav'n-ly teach-ing turn,
With sim-ple, child-like hearts!


---
Author: Bernard D. Barton (1784-1849)
Tune: Paraclete
