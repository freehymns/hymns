When He Cometh

Verse 1:
When He com-eth, when He com-eth/:\n:/ To make up His jew-els,
All His jew-els, pre-cious jew-els,/:\n:/ His loved and His own:

Refrain:
Like the stars of the morn-ing,
His_ bright crown a-dorn-ing,
They shall shine in their beau-ty,/:\n:/ Bright gems for His crown.

Verse 2:
He will gath-er, He will gath-er/:\n:/ The gems for His king-dom;
All the pure ones, all the bright ones,/:\n:/ His loved and His own.

Verse 3:
Lit-tle chil-dren, lit-tle chil-dren,/:\n:/ Who love their Re-deem-er,
Are the jew-els, pre-cious jew-els,/:\n:/ His loved and His own.


---
Author: William Cushing, 1856
Tune: Jewels
