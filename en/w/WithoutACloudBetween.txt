Without a Cloud Between

Verse 1:
"With-out a cloud be-tween"
To see Him face to face,
Not struck with dire a-maze-ment dumb,
But tri-umph-ing in grace.

Verse 2:
"With-out a cloud be-tween"
To see Him "as He is;"
Oh! who can tell the height of joy,
The full trans-port-ing bliss.

Verse 3:
"With-out a cloud be-tween"
My long-ing spir-it waits
For that sweet hour from which my soul
Its high-est glo-ry dates.

Verse 4:
"With-out a cloud be-tween"
Lord Je-sus, haste the day,
The morn-ing bright, with-out a cloud,
And chase our tears a-way!


---
Albert Midlane (1825-1909)
Tune: Dennis
