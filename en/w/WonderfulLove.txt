Wonderful Love

Verse 1:
O, have you not heard of that won-der-ful love,
That flows from God’s heart_ so free,
Which led Him to give for a per-ish-ing world=

Poor sin-ners un-done,_ and sin-ful, and lost,

Then will you not prove_ this won-der-ful love,
That flows from God’s heart_ so free,
