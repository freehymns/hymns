Watch for Me, for I Am Coming

Verse 1:
Deep-er grows the dark-ness 'round us
Fierc-er grows the strif;
Still our ab-sent Lord o-bey-ing,
Yield we but with life.

Refrain:
"Watch for Me, for I am com-ing,"
This His prom-ise still;
And our hearts re-spond with glad-ness,
"Bless-èd Lord, we will!"

Verse 2:
Sa-tan's might-y hosts as-sail us,
Spread-ing snares a-round;
Striv-ing by all arts to drive us

Verse 3:
Hearts grow faint, and eyes grow wea-ry,
Watch-ing through the night;
But we know a day of splen-dor
Dawns with morn-ing light.

Verse 4:
Then the wait-ing time is end-ed,
Then the bat-tle won;
