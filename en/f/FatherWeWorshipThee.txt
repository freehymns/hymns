Father, We Worship Thee

Verse 1:
Fa-ther, we wor-ship Thee,
Through Thy be-lov-èd Son;
And, by the Spir-it, now draw near
Be-fore Thy ho-ly throne.

Verse 2:
We bless Thee Thou art Light,
Right-eous and true art Thou;
Ho-ly and rev-er-end Thy name,
Our hearts be-fore Thee bow.

Verse 3:
We bless Thee Thou art Love,
How vast that match-less grace,
Whose breadth and length and height and depth
No fi-nite mind can trace!

Verse 4:
We bless Thee most of all
For Him Who Thee un-veiled;
Whose pre-cious blood re-demp-tion wrought,
And thus Thy heart re-vealed.

Verse 5:
For what Thou art, we praise
And wor-ship and a-dore:
To Fa-ther, Son and Spir-it be
The glo-ry ev-er-more!


---
Author: Alfred P. Gibbs (1890-1967)
Tune: Trentham
