Father And God, In Grateful Love

Verse 1:
Fa-ther and God, in grate-ful love
We bow be-fore Thy face,
While for They gift un-speak-a-ble
Our souls o'er-flow with praise;
Thine on-ly Son, Thy heart's de-light
Far back, 'ere time be-gan,
Thou in Thy bound-less love didst give,
To die for ru-ined man.

Verse 2:
Thou gav-est Him, well know-ing all
That lay be-fore Him here -
The suf-f'ring sore, the thorn-y crown,
The cross, the cru-el spear -
And in that hour of woe su-preme
When Je-sus bore our sin,
God's pa-tient, ho-ly, suf-f'ring Lamb,
Thou didst for-sake Him then.

Verse 3:
Fa-ther, this mys-ter-y of love
Must all our praise ex-cel;
No hu-man, no an-gel-ic tongue
Its won-drous depths can tell;
For what were we that Thou on us
Such love shouldst ev-er pour?
Our souls, with joy and won-der filled,
Fa-ther and Son a-dore!


---
Author: Edward Whyte
Tune: GreenHill
