From Heav'n Above to Earth

Verse 1:
"From Heav'n a-bove to earth I came,
To bear good news to ev-'ry name;
Glad tid-ings of great joy I bring"
These words we heard the an-gels sing.

Verse 2:
"To you this night is born a child
Of Ma-ry, cho-sen mo ther mild;
This lit-tle Child, of low-ly birth,
Shall be the joy of all the earth."

Verse 3:
"’Tis Christ our God, who from on high
Has heard your sad and bit-ter cry;
From bon-dage and from mis-er-y,
He will your full sal-va-tion be."

Verse 4:
Wel-come to earth, you no-ble guest,
Through whom men ev-'ry-where are blest!
Who from our sins shall set us free,
We wor--ship you on bend-ed knee!

Verse 5:
Glo-ry to God in high-est Heav'n,
Who un-to man His Son has given!
As an-gels sang with pi-ous mirth
Still sing the news through-out the earth.

---
Topic: Christmas
Tune: VomHimmelHoch
Original Language: German
Author: Martin Luther (1535)
Translator: Catherine Winkworth (1835)
