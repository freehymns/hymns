From His Bright Throne Eternal

Verse 1:
From His bright throne e-ter-nal,
He came to earth be-low;
From God-head's full-est glo-ry
He came to bear our woe

Verse 2:
He came to scorn and ha-tred,
He came to shame and loss,
He came to be the Vic-tim,
To die up-on the cross!

Verse 3:
He suf-fered in the dark-ness,
With none His grief to share;
And though He looked for com-fort,
No com-fort-ers were there.

Verse 4:
A-lone a-mid the dark-ness,
He gave Him-self for us;
Oh might-y love of Je-sus,
To die and suf-fer thus!

Verse 5:
Lord Je-sus now as-cend-ed,
En-throned in maj-es-ty;
We wor-ship and a-dore Thee,
Thou Lamb of Cal-va-ry!


---
Author: A. Stevenson
Author, verse 5: Alfred P. Gibbs (1890-1967)
Tune: Sacrifice
