Finished Is the Battle Now

Refrain A:
Hal-le-lu-jah! Hal-le-lu-jah!

Verse 1:
Fin-ished is the bat-tle now:
Fit-ly crowned the Vic-tor's brow!
Fin-ished sad-ness! Sing with glad-ness,

Refrain B:
Hal-le-lu-jah!

Verse 2:
Af-ter death on Him be-fell,
Je-sus Christ hath con-quered hell.
Earth is sing-ing, Heav'n is ring-ing,

Verse 3:
On the third morning He a-rose,
Bright with vic-t'ry o'er His foes.
Sing we laud-ing, and ap-plaud-ing,

Verse 4:
He hath closed hell's braz-en door,
Heav'n is op-en ev-er-more!
Ban-ished sad-ness! Wel-comed glad-ness,

Verse 5:
By Thy wounds we call on Thee,
Now from death to set us free,
That our liv-ing be thanks-giv-ing!

---
Topic: Easter
Origianl Language: Latin
Author: Traditional (12th Century)
Translator: John M. Neale (1851)
