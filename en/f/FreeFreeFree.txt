Free, Free, Free

Verse 1:
Hark! sin-ner, hark! we have ti-dings so true,
Ti-dings of par-don and bless-ings for you!
God in His Word says that Christ on the tree,
Died for guilt-y sin-ners, and “Sal-va-tion is free.”

Refrain:
Hear the news, sin-ner, free! free! free!
Why not be-lieve it? ‘Tis good news for thee.
Je-sus the Just One has died on the tree,
Died for guilt-y sin-ners, and “Sal-va-tion is free.”

Verse 2:
Guilt-y you are, yet you know ver-y well
Je-sus has suf-fered to save you from hell;
Though now con-demned, jus-ti-fied you may be,
Je-sus paid the ran-som and “Sal-va-tion is free.”

Verse 3:
Trust not in “do-ing” it can-not a-vail,
Good res-o-lu-tions and works can but fail;
“Grace, grace a-lone,” is the saved sin-ner’s plea,
“Not of works” the Scrip-tures say, “Sal-va-tion is free.”

Verse 4:
Trust not in feel-ings, your heart is de-praved,
Trust on-ly Je-sus, and you shall be saved;
Tears of re-pent-ance though re-al they may be,
Ne’er can pur-chase heav-en, for “Sal-va-tion is free.”


---
Author: T. D. W. Muir
Tune: FreeFreeFree
