Zion Stands By Hills Surrounded

Verse 1:
Zi-on stands by hills sur-round-ed,
Zi-on, kept by pow’r di-vine;
All her foes shall be con-found-ed,
Though the world in arms com-bine;
Hap-py Zi-on, what a fa-vored lot is thine!
Hap-py Zi-on, what a fa-vored lot is thine!

Verse 2:
Ev-'ry hu-man tie may per-ish;

But no chang-es can at-tend Je-ho-vah’s love.

Verse 3:
In the fur-nace God may prove thee,
Thence to bring thee forth more bright,
But can nev-er cease to love thee;
Thou art pre-cious in His sight;
God is with thee, God, thine ev-er-last-ing Light.
God is with thee, God, thine ev-er-last-ing Light.
