In Remembrance Of Thee

Verse 1:
Lord Je-sus in Thy pre-cious name,
And in that name a-lone;
At Thy re-quest we glad-ly meet,
Thy Lord-ship here would own.

Verse 2:
As on that dark be-tray-al night,
Thou didst this feast or-dain
We too, the bread and cup would take,
Thy death, Lord, thus pro-claim.

Verse 3:
The bread, Thy bod-y doth por-tray;
The cup, Thy pre-cious blood;
By which our sin was put a-way,
Our peace was made with God.

Verse 4:
The Host art Thou, O bless-èd Lord,
Thy hon-ored guests are we;
With grate-ful and a-dor-ing hearts
We would re-mem-ber Thee!

Verse 5:
Lord Je-sus, Whom un-seen, we love,
As thus we muse on Thee;
We none would see, save Thee a-lone,
Thou Man of Cal-va-ry.

---
Author: Alfred P. Gibbs (1890-1967)
Tune: Edwin
