In My Heart There Rings a Melody

Verse 1:
I have a song that Je-sus gave me,
It was sent from Heav’n a-bove;
There nev-er was a sweet-er mel-o-dy,
’Tis the mel-o-dy of love.

Refrain:
In my heart there rings a mel-o-dy,
There rings a mel-o-dy with Heav-en’s har-mo-ny;
In my heart there rings a mel-o-dy,
There rings a mel-o-dy of love.

Verse 2:
I love the Christ who died on Cal-v’ry,
For He washed my sins a-way;
He put with-in my heart a mel-o-dy,
And I know it’s there to stay.

Verse 3:
’Twill be my end-less theme in glo-ry,
With the an-gels I will sing;
’Twill be a song with glor-ious har-mo-ny,
When the courts of Heav-en ring.


---
Author: Elton Menno Roth, 1924
