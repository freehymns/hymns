Unto Him Who Hath Loved Us

Verse 1:
With harps and with vi-ols, there stands a great throng
In the pres-ence of Je-sus, and sings this new song:

Refrain:
Un-to Him who hath loved us and washed us from sin,
Un-to Him be the glo-ry for ev-er. A-men.

Verse 2:
All these once were sin-ners, de-filed in His sight,
Now ar-rayed in pure gar-ments in praise they u-nite:

Verse 3:
He mak-eth the reb-el a priest and a king,
He hath bought us and taught us this new song to sing:

Verse 4:
How help-less and hope-less we sin-ners had been,
If He nev-er had loved us till cleansed from our sin:

Verse 5:
A-loud in His prais-es our voic-es shall ring,
So that oth-ers be-liev-ing, this new song shall sing:


---
Author: Arthur T. Pierson (1837-1911)
Tune: TheNewSong
