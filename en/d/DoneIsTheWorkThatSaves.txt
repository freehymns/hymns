Done Is The Work That Saves!

Verse 1:
Done is the work that saves!
Once and for-ev-er done;
Fin-ished the right-eous-ness
That clothes th'un-right-eous one.
The love that bless-es us be-low
Is flow-ing free-ly to us now.

Verse 2:
The sac-ri-fice is o'er,
The veil is rent in twain,
The mer-cy-seat is red
With blood of vic-tim slain.
Why stand we then with-out, in fear?
The blood of Christ in-vites us near.

Verse 3:
The gate is o-pen wide;
The new and liv-ing way
Is clear, and free, and bright,
With love, and peace, and day.
In-to the ho-li-est we come,
Our pres-ent and our end-less home.

Verse 4:
En-throned in maj-es-ty,
The High Priest sits with-in;
His pre-cious blood, once shed,
Has made and keeps us clean;
With bold-ness let us now draw near;
That blood has ban-ished ev-'ry fear.

Verse 5:
Then to the Lamb once slain
Be glo-ry, praise, and pow'r,
Who died and lives a-gain,
Who liv-eth ev-er-more;
Who loved us, cleansed us by His blood,
And made us kings and priests to God!


---
Author: Horatius Bonar (1808-1889)
Tune: StJohn
