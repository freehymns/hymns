Ding Dong Merrily on High

Verse 1:
Ding dong! Mer-ri-ly on high,
In Heav’n the bells are ring-ing.
Ding dong! Ver-i-ly the sky,
Is riv’n with an-gel sing-ing.

Verse 2:
E’en so here on earth be-low,
Let earth ring out in her glee.
God to man-kind doth be-stow,
His bound-less love and mer-cy.

Verse 3:
For this chi-ld of His love,
The wond-rous gift He gave us,
Thank the God who reigns a-bove.
Christ came to earth to save us.

Refrain:
Glo-o-o-o-o-o-ri-a
Hos-an-nah in the high-est!
