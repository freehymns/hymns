Death and Judgment Are Behind Us

Verse 1:
Death and judg-ment are be-hind us,
Grace and glor-y are be-fore:
All the bil-lows rolled o'er Je-sus,
There ex-pend-ed all their pow'r.

Verse 2:
"First-fruits" of the res-ur-rect-ion,
He is ris-en from the tomb;
Now we stand in new cre-a-tion,
Free from an-y trace of doom.

Verse 3:
Je-sus died, and we died with Him.
"Bur-ied" in His grave we lay,
One with Him in res-ur-rect-ion,
Now "in Him" in heav-en's day.

Verse 4:
We a-wait the full re-dempt-ion,
When the Ris-en One shall come;
And our bod-ies shall be trans-formed,
To the pat-tern of His own.

---
Topic: Easter
Tune: Dijon
Author: Mrs. J. A. Trench (1843-1925)
Edits: Updated for clarity.
