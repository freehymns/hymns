King Of Kings And Lord Of Lords

Verse 1:
King of kings and Lord of lords,
Oh, how rich these glor-ious words!
Ti-tles high and bound-less fame
Now en-hance the Sav-ior's name.

Verse 2:
He who once was crowned with thorn
Crowns of glo-ry now a-dorn;
Je-sus sits up-on the throne,
Hosts His tri-umph glad-ly own!

Verse 3:
Bless-èd 'tis to see Him there,
Cen-ter of that glo-ry fair;
Thus our high-est praise we bring,
Fall-ing pros-trate, wor-ship-ping.

Verse 4:
Now with-in the Fa-ther's house
Well we know Him and re-joice;
Glad that He, e'en now, should share
All the Fa-ther's glo-ry there.

Verse 5:
Soon will He ap-pear a-gain,
Then His saints with Him shall reign;
Ech-o far the glor-ious words:
King of kings and Lord of lords.


---
Author: J. Wilson Smith
Tune: PleyelsHymn
