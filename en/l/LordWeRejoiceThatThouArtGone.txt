Lord, We Rejoice That Thou Art Gone

Verse 1:
Lord, we re-joice that Thou art gone
To sit up-on Thy Fa-ther's throne;
Thy path of shame and suf-f'ring o'er,
Thy heart shall grieve and mourn no more.

Verse 2:
O cross of Christ! O Cal-va-ry!
What place can be com-pared with thee,
Where God's own Son was cru-ci-fied,
And for our sins a ran-som died?

Verse 3:
We love to look with-in the tomb
Thy death has robbed of all it's gloom;
The stone for-ev-er rolled a-way;
Thy death the pow'r of death did slay.

Verse 4:
We joy to see Thee, Lord a-rise
Tri-um-phant through the open-ing skies;
And hear all heav'n u-nit-ed own
Thee wor-thy to as-cend the throne.

Verse 5:
Lord, now we wait for Thee to come
And take us to Thy Fa-ther's home;
Oh what ec-stat-ic joy 'twill be
To spend e-ter-ni-ty with Thee!


---
Author: James G. Deck (1807-1884)
Tune: Maryton
