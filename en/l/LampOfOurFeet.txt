Lamp Of Our Feet

Verse 1:
Lamp of our feet! where-by we trace
Our path, when wont to stray;
Stream from the Fount of heav'n-ly grace!
Brook by the trav-'ler's way!

Verse 2:
Bread of our souls! where-on we feed;
True man-na from on high!
Our guide, our chart, where-in we read
Of realms be-yond the sky.

Verse 3:
Pil-lar of fire, through watch-es dark!
Or rad-iant cloud by day!
When waves would whelm our toss-ing bark,
Our an-chor and our stay!

Verse 4:
Child-hood's pre-cep-tor! man-hood's trust!
Old age-'s firm al-ly!
Our hope, when we go down to dust,
Of im-mor-tal-i-ty!


---
Author: Bernard D. Barton (1784-1849)
Tune: Paraclete
