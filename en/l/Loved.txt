Loved

Verse 1:
Loved! then the way will not be drear;
For One we know is ev-er near,
Prov-ing it to our hearts so clear
That we are loved.

Verse 2:
Loved with an ev-er-last-ing love,
By Him who left His home a-bove,
To bring us life, and light, and love,
Be-cause He loved.

Verse 3:
Loved, when our sky is cloud-ed o’er,
And days of sor-row press us sore;
Still will we trust Him ev-er-more,
For we are loved.

Verse 4:
Loved, when we leave our na-tive soil,
In hea-then lands to live and toil;
Un-der His shad-ows naught can foil—
Still we are loved.

Verse 5:
Loved when we sing the glad new song
To Christ, for whom we’ve wait-ed long,
With all the hap-py, ran-somed throng
For ev-er loved.


---
Author: Grace Pennell, 1916
Tune: Memorial
