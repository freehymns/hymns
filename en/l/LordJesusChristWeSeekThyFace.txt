Lord Jesus Christ, We Seek Thy Face

Verse 1:
Lord Je-sus Christ, we seek Thy face;
With-in the veil we bow the knee;
Oh, let Thy glo-ry fill the place,
And bless us while we wait on Thee.

Verse 2:
We thank Thee for the pre-cious blood
That purged our sins and brought us nigh;
All cleansed and sanc-ti-fied to God,
Thy ho-ly name to mag-ni-fy.

Verse 3:
Shut in with Thee far, far a-bove
The rest-less world that wars be-low;
We seek to learn and prove Thy love,
Thy wis-dom and Thy grace to know.

Verse 4:
The brow that once with thorns was bound,
Thy hands, Thy side, we fain would see;
Draw near, Lord Je-sus; glo-ry crowned,
And bless us while we wait on Thee.


---
Author: Alexander Stewart
Tune: Retreat
