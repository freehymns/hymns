Lord Jesus! When I Think Of Thee

Verse 1:
Lord Je-sus! when I think of Thee,
Of all Thy love and grace,
My spir-it longs and fain would see
Thy beau-ty, face to face.

Verse 2:
And though the wil-der-ness I tread,
A bar-ren, thirst-y ground,
With thorns and bri-ars o-ver-spread,
Where foes and snares a-bound;

Verse 3:
Yet in Thy love such depths I see,
My soul o'er-flows with praise,
Con-tents it-self, while, Lord, to Thee
A joy-ful song I raise.

Verse 4:
My Lord, my Life, my Rest, my Shield,
My Rock, my Food, my Light;
Each thought of Thee doth con-stant yield
Un-chang-ing, fresh de-light.

Verse 5:
My Sav-iour, keep my spir-it stayed,
Hard fol-l'wing aft-er Thee;
Till I, in robes of white ar-rayed,
Thy face in glo-ry see.
