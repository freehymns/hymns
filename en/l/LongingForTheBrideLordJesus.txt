Longing For The Bride, Lord Jesus

Verse 1:
Long-ing for the bride, Lord Je-sus,
Of Thy heart,
To be with Thee in the glo-ry,
Where Thou art:
Love so ground-less, grace so bound-less
Wins my heart. Wins my heart.

Verse 2:
When Thy blood-bought church, Lord Je-sus,
Is com-plete;
When each soul is safe-ly land-ed
At Thy feet;
What a sto-ry in the glo-ry
She'll re-peat! She'll re-peat!

Verse 3:
O to praise Thee there Lord Je-sus,
Ev-er-more!
O to grieve and wan-der from Thee
Nev-er more!
Earth's sad sto-ry, closed in glo-ry,
On yon shore! On yon shore!

Verse 4:
Then Thy church will be, Lord Je-sus
The dis-play
Of Thy rich-est grace and kind-ness
In that day;
Mark-ing pag-es, won-drous stag-es,
O'er earth's way. O'er earth's way.


---
Author: Miss C. A. Wellesley
Tune: Satisfied
