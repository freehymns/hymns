Lord Jesus, To Tell Of Thy Love

Verse 1:
Lord Je-sus, to tell of Thy love
Our souls shall for ev-er de-light,
And sing of Thy glo-ry a-bove,
In prais-es by day and by night.
Wher-ev-er we fol-low Thee, Lord,
Ad-mir-ing, a-dor-ing, we see
That love which was strong-er than death,
Flow out with-out lim-it, and free.

Verse 2:
De-scend-ing from glo-ry on high,
With men Thy de-light was to dwell,
Con-tent-ed, our Sure-ty to die,
Nor e're didst the vil-est re-pel.
En-dur-ing the grief and the shame,
Thou bar-est our sins on the cross,
Oh! who would not boast of this love,
And count the world's glo-ry but loss?


---
Author: T. Hawels (1734-1820)
Tune: Contrast
Source: https://hymnary.org/hymn/HoWR1950/255.  This is a significant revision of https://hymnary.org/hymn/HSSR1843/95
