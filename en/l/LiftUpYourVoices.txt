Lift Up Your Voices

Verse 1:
Lift up your ju-bi-lant voic-es,
All ye glad na-tions and sing;
Je-sus the Sav-ior is ris-en,
Je-sus your Sav-ior and king.

Refrain:
Je-sus liv-eth! He hath ris-en,
Sund-ered the tomb and con-quer'd the grave;_
Might-y Sav-ior, live for-ev-er,
Je-sus is king and He doth save.

Verse 2:
Lift up your ju-bi-lant voic-es,
Greet Him with prais-es of joy;
Give to Him glo-ry and hon-or
Grand-est of an-thems em-ploy.

Verse 3:
Lift up your ju-bi-lant voic-es,
Break forth with sweet-est of songs;
Je-sus the Sav-ior is ris-en,
Glo-ry to Him now be-longs.

---
Topic: Easter
Author: Minnie A. Greiner (1900)
