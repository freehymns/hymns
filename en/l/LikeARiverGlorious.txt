Like a riv-er glor-ious, is God’s per-fect peace,

Stayed up-on Je-ho-vah, hearts are ful-ly blest
Find-ing, as He prom-ised, per-fect peace and rest.

Nev-er foe can fol-low, nev-er trai-tor stand;
