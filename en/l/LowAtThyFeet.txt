Low At Thy Feet

Verse 1:
Low at Thy feet, Lord Je-sus,
This is the place for me;
Here I have learned deep les-sons,
Truth that has made me free.
Here I have learned deep les-sons,
Truth that has made me free.

Verse 2:
Free from my-self, Lord Je-sus,
Free from the ways of men;
Chains of thought that once bound me,
Ne’er can en-slave a-gain.
Chains of thought that once bound me,
Ne’er can en-slave a-gain.

Verse 3:
On-ly Thy-self, Lord Je-sus,
Con-quered my way-ward will;
But for Thy love con-strain-ing,
I had been way-ward still.
But for Thy love con-strain-ing,
I had been way-ward still.
