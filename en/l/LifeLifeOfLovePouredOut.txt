Life, Life Of Love Poured Out

Verse 1:
Life, life of love poured out fra-grant and ho-ly!
Life, 'mid rude thorns of earth, stain-less and sweet!
Life, whence God's face of love, glor-ious but low-ly,
Shines forth to bow us, Lord, low at Thy feet!

Verse 2:
Grief, grief of love that drew hate's ev-'ry ar-row!
Grief that Thy suf-f'ring heart on-ly could meet!
Grief, whence Thy face of love, shin-ing in sor-row,
Draws us, a-dor-ing, Lord, low at Thy feet!

Verse 3:
Death, death of strick-en love, wrath's sea ex-plor-ing!
Death, Life's mys-te-rious death; Deep meet-ing deep!
Death, whence Thy burst-ing heart fills ours out-pour-ing
All, all in wor-ship, Lord, low at Thy feet!

Author: F. Allaben
Tune: Consolator
