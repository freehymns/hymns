Living For Jesus

Verse 1:
Liv-ing for Je-sus, a life that is true,
Striv-ing to please Him in all that I do;
Yield-ing al-le-giance, glad heart-ed and free,
This is the path-way of bless-ing for me.

Refrain:
O Je-sus, Lord and Sav-ior,
I give my-self to Thee,
For Thou, in Thy a-tone-ment,
Didst give Thy-self for me.
I own no oth-er mast-er,
My heart shall be Thy throne.
My life I give, hence-forth to live,
O Christ, for Thee a-lone.

Verse 2:
Liv-ing for Je-sus who died in my place,
Bear-ing on Cal-v'ry my sin and dis-grace;
Such love con-strains me to an-swer His call,
Fol-low His lead-ing and give Him my all.

Verse 3:
Liv-ing for Je-sus, wher-ev-er I am,
Do-ing each du-ty in His ho-ly name;
Will-ing to suf-fer af-flic-tion and loss,
Deem-ing each tri-al a part of my cross.

Verse 4:
Liv-ing for Je-sus through earth’s lit-tle while,
My dear-est treas-ure, the light of His smile;
Seek-ing the lost ones He died to re-deem,
Bring-ing the wea-ry to find rest in Him.
