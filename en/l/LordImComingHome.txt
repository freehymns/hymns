Lord, I’m Coming Home

Verse 1:
I’ve wan-dered far a-way from God,
Now I’m com-ing home;
The paths of sin too long I’ve trod,
Lord, I’m com-ing home.

Refrain:
Com-ing home, com-ing home,
Nev-er-more to roam,
O-pen wide Thine arms of love,
Lord, I’m com-ing home.

Verse 2:
I’ve wast-ed ma-ny pre-cious years,
Now I’m com-ing home;
I now re-pent with bit-ter tears,
Lord, I’m com-ing home.

Verse 3:
I’m tired of sin and stray-ing, Lord,
Now I’m com-ing home;
I’ll trust Thy love, be-lieve Thy Word,
Lord, I’m com-ing home.

Verse 4:
My soul is sick, my heart is sore,
Now I’m com-ing home;
My strength re-new, my hope re-store,
Lord, I’m com-ing home.

Verse 5:
My on-ly hope, my on-ly plea,
Now I’m com-ing home;
That Je-sus died, and died for me.
Lord, I’m com-ing home.

Verse 6:
I need His cleans-ing blood, I know,
Now I’m com-ing home;
O wash me whit-er than the snow,
Lord, I’m com-ing home.


---
Author: William James Kirkpatrick (1838–1921)
Corrections: Verse 2, line 3, Add the word "bitter"
