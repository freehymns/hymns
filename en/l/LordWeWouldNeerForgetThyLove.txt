Lord, We Would Ne'Er Forget Thy Love

Verse 1:
Lord, we would ne'er for-get Thy love,
Who hast re-deemed us by Thy blood;
And now, as our High Priest a-bove
Dost in-ter-cede for us with God.

Verse 2:
Lord, we would not for-get the pain,
The blood-like sweat, the shame-ful tree,
The wrath Thy soul did once sus-tain,
From sin and death to set us free.

Verse 3:
We would re-mem-ber we are one
With ev-'ry saint that loves Thy name;
U-nit-ed to Thee on the throne,
Our life, our hope, our Lord the same.

Verse 4:
Here, in the bro-ken bread and wine,
We hear Thee say, "re-mem-ber Me!
I gave My life to ran-som thine,
I bore the wrath to set thee free."

Verse 5:
Lord, we are Thine, we praise Thy love,
One with Thy saints, all one in Thee;
We would, un-til we meet a-bove,
In all our ways re-mem-ber Thee.


---
Author: James G. Deck (1807-1884)
Tune: Rockingham
