Lord Jesus We Worship

Verse 1:
Lord Je-sus we wor-ship and bow at Thy feet,
And give Thee the glo-ry, the hon-or that's meet.
While through Thee, O Sav-ior, our prais-es as-cend
And join in the cho-rus that nev-er shall end.
