Christ The Lord Will Come Again

Verse 1:
Christ the Lord will come a-gain,
None shall wait for Him in vain;
I shall then His glo-ry see,
Christ will come and call for me.

Verse 2:
Then, when the Arch-an-gel’s voice
Calls the sleep-ing saints to rise,
Ris-ing mil-lions shall pro-claim
Bless-ings on the Sav-ior's name.

Verse 3:
This is our re-deem-ing God!
Ran-somed hosts will shout a-loud:
Praise, e-ter-nal praise, be giv’n
To the Lord of earth and heav’n!


---
Author: Joseph Swain (1761-1794)
Tune: SamariaNoRefrain
