Christian Brethren, Ere We Part

Verse 1:
Chris-tian breth-ren, ere we part,
Let us each, with grate-ful heart,
Once more to our Fa-ther raise
Our u-nit-ed hymn of praise.

Verse 2:
Here per-haps we meet no more,
But we seek a bright-er shore,
Where, a-bove all sin and pain,
Breth-ren, we shall meet a-gain.

Verse 3:
To the Tri-une God of heav'n,
Love and praise be ev-er giv'n;
Here, and by the hosts a-bove,
End-less praise, a-dor-ing love.


---
Author: R. White
Tune: Innocents
