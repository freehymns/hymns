Crowns Of Glory, Ever Bright

Verse 1:
Crowns of glo-ry, ev-er bright,
Rest up-on the Vic-tor's head;
Crowns of glo-ry are His right,
His, who liv-eth and was dead.
Je-sus fought and won the day;
Such a fight was nev-er fought;
Well His peo-ple now may say,
See what God, our God, has wrought.
Well His peo-ple now may say,
See what God, our God, has wrought.

Verse 2:
He sub-dued the pow'rs of hell;
In the fight He stood a-lone;
All His foes be-fore Him fell,
By His sin-gle arm o'er-thrown.
They did fall to rise no more;
Fi-nal is the foe's de-feat:
Je-sus tri-umphed by His pow'r,
And His tri-umph is com-plete.
Je-sus tri-umphed by His pow'r,
And His tri-umph is com-plete.

Verse 3:
His the fight, the ar-duous toil,
His the hon-ors of the day;
His the glo-ry and the spoil:
Je-sus bears them all a-way!
Now pro-claim His deeds a-far;
Fill the world with His re-nown;
His a-lone the vic-tor's car;
His the ev-er-last-ing crown.
His a-lone the vic-tor's car;
