Christ The Lord Is Ris'n Indeed

Verse 1:
Christ the Lord is ris'n in-deed,
Hal-le-lu-jah!
He has met His peo-ple's need,
Hal-le-lu-jah!
Borne their sins up-on the cross,
Hal-le-lu-jah!
Suf-fered death and shame and loss,
Hal-le-lu-jah!

Verse 2:
Yes, He took the bit-ter cup,
Hal-le-lu-jah!
To the dregs He drank it up;
Hal-le-lu-jah!
Gives the cup of bless-ing now,
Hal-le-lu-jah!
Wor-thy, Je-sus, Lord, art Thou!
Hal-le-lu-jah!

Verse 3:
Here He came from realms of light,
Hal-le-lu-jah!
Passed thro' dark-ness, gloom and night,
Hal-le-lu-jah!
Fath-omed all the depth of woe,
Hal-le-lu-jah!
Tri-umphed o-ver ev-'ry foe,
Hal-le-lu-jah!

Verse 4:
God, that Ho-ly One who gave,
Hal-le-lu-jah!
Could not leave Him in the grave,
Hal-le-lu-jah!
Raised Him to the high-est height,
Hal-le-lu-jah!
Crowned Him there with glo-ry bright,
Hal-le-lu-jah!

Verse 5:
From the glo-ry He will come,
Hal-le-lu-jah!
First to take His peo-ple home,
Hal-le-lu-jah!
Soon with them o'er death to reign,
Hal-le-lu-jah!
Come, Lord Je-sus, come, A-men.
Hal-le-lu-jah!
