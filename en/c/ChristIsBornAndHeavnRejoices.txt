Christ is Born and Heav’n Rejoices

Verse 1:
Christ is born, and Heav’n re-joic-es,
Jud-ah’s plain is bathed in light;
Thous-and, thou-sand harps and voic-es
Break the si-lence of the night.

Refrain:
Glo-ry in the high-est, glo-ry,
Peace on earth, good will to men.

Verse 2:
To the lost He brings sal-va-tion,
Free-dom to the cap-tive slave;
Peace a-mid death’s de-so-la-tion,
Vic-t'ry o’er the boast-ing grave.

Verse 3:
Christ is born, O won-drous sto-ry!
Lord of life, yet born to die;
Sor-row’s child, yet King of Glo-ry:
Born to rule and reign on high.

Verse 4:
Roy-al babe, though few en-throne Him,
Few their grate-ful off'r-ings bring;
All the tribes of earth shall own Him
Prince of Peace, cre-a-tion’s king.

---
Topic: Christmas
Tune: RegentSquare
Author: Arthur A. Graley (1865)
