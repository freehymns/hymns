Crowned With The Victor'S Crown

Verse 1:
Crowned with the Vic-tor's crown,
Je-sus on high we see;
He won that place of high re-nown
On Cal-v'ry's cru-el tree.

Verse 2:
He bore our bit-ter grief,
Be-neath our sor-row sank,
His bruis-ing brought us sweet re-lief,
Our cup of wrath He drank.

Verse 3:
Our sins and fol-lies bound
The thorns up-on His brow;
Be-hold Him there with hon-or crowned
The King of glo-ry now!

Verse 4:
And though on high en-throned,
His pres-ence here we claim,
By Him as breth-ren, chil-dren owned,
Graced with His wor-thy name.


---
Author: J. G. Wheeler
Tune: StThomas
