Coming to the Garden

Verse 1:
Com-ing to the gar-den at the break-ing of the day,
Lov-ing friends of Je-sus found the stone was rolled a-way,
Know-ing not the mean-ing of the Sav-ior's emp-ty tomb,
See-ing not the les-son in the lil-y's bloom.

Refrain:
Bloom, lil-ies, bloom, by the Sav-ior's emp-ty tomb.
He is ris-en; Hal-le-lu-jah!
Light e-clips-es dark-ness; Death has lost fin-al-i-ty;

Verse 2:
Com-ing to the gar-den, weep-ing Ma-ry sought her Lord,
And His ten-der greet-ing was her won-der-ful re-ward;
So we come to meet Him in the gar-den of our prayer,
Know-ing with as-sur-ance we will find Him there.

Verse 3:
Com-ing to the gar-den, when this earth-ly life shall end,
When to bowers of glo-ry all His ran-somed shall as-cend,

Praise to our Re-deem-er, hon-or, pow'r and might.
