Once For All

Verse 1:
Free from the law, O hap-py con-di-tion,
Je-sus has bled and there is re-mis-sion,
Cursed by the law and bruised by the fall,
Grace hath re-deemed us once for all.

Refrain:
Once for all, O sin-ner, re-ceive it,
Once for all, O broth-er, be-lieve it;
Cling to the cross, the bur-den will fall,
Christ hath re-deemed us once for all.

Verse 2:
Now we are free, there’s no con-dem-na-tion,
Je-sus pro-vides a per-fect sal-va-tion.
Come un-to Me, O hear His sweet call,
Come, and He saves us once for all.

Verse 3:
Chil-dren of God, O glo-ri-ous call-ing,
Sure-ly His grace will keep us from fall-ing;
Pass-ing from death to life at His call;
Bless-èd sal-va-tion once for all.


---
Author: Philip Paul Bliss (1838–1876)
