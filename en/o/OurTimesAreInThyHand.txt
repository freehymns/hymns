Our Times Are In Thy Hand

Verse 1:
Our times are in thy hand;
Fa-ther, we wish them there!
Our life, our souls, our all we leave
En-tire-ly to Thy care.

Verse 2:
Our times are in thy hand,
What-ev-er they may be;
Pleas-ing or pain-ful, dark or bright,
As best may seem to Thee.

Verse 3:
Our times are in thy hand;
Why should we doubt or fear?
Our Fa-ther's hand will nev-er cause
His child a nee-dless tear.

Verse 4:
Our times are in thy hand,
Je-sus the cru-ci-fied!
The hand our man-y sins have pierced
Is now our guard and guide.

Verse 5:
Our times are in thy hand,
Oh Lord, our Ad-vo-cate!
Nor is that hand out-stretched in vain,
For us to sup-pli-cate.


---
Author: William F. Lloyd (1791-1853)
Tune: Silchester
