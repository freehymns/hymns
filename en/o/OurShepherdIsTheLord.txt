Our Shepherd Is The Lord

Verse 1:
Our Shep-herd is the Lord,
The liv-ing Lord who died:
With all His ful-ness can af-ford
We are sup-plied.
He rich-ly feeds our souls
With bless-ings from a-bove;
And leads us where the riv-er rolls
Of end-less love.

Verse 2:
Our souls He doth re-store,
And keeps us in His way;
He makes our cup of joy run o'er,
From day to day;
Through love so full, so deep,
A-noint-ed is our head;
Mer-cy and good-ness us shall keep,
Wher-e'er we tread.

Verse 3:
When faith and hope shall cease,
And love a-bide a-lone,
Then shall we see Him face to face,
And know as known:
Still shall we lift our voice,
His praise our song shall be;
And we shall in His love re-joice
Who set us free.


---
Author: John Beaumont (1762–1822)
Tune: Priory
Variant: My Shepherd is the Lamb
