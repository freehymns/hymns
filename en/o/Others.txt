Verse 1:
Lord, help me live from day to day
In such a self-for-get-ful way
That e-ven when I kneel to pray
My prayer shall be for oth-ers.

Refrain:
Oth-ers, Lord, yes oth-ers,
Let this my mot-to be,
Help me to live for oth-ers,
That I may live like Thee.

Verse 2:
Help me in all the work I do
To ev-er be sin-cere and true
And know that all I’d do for You
Must needs be done for oth-ers.

Verse 3:
Let Self be cru-ci-fied and slain
And bur-ied deep, nor rise a-gain,: 
And may all ef-forts be in vain,
Un-less they be for oth-ers.

Verse 4:
And when my work on earth is done,
And my new work in Heav’n’s be-gun,
May I for-get the crown I’ve won,
While think-ing still of oth-ers.


---
Author: Charles D. Meigs, 1902
