One Spirit With The Lord;

Verse 1:
"One spir-it" with the Lord;
Oh, bless-èd, won-drous word!
What heav'n-ly light, what pow'r di-vine,
Doth that sweet word af-ford!
"One spir-it" with the Lord:
Je-sus, the glo-ri-fied,
Es-teems the Church for which He bled,
His bod-y and His bride.

Verse 2:
And though by storms as-sailed,
And though by tri-als pressed,
Him-self our Life, He bears us up
Right on-ward to the rest.
There we shall drink the stream
Of end-less bliss a-bove:
There we shall know, with-out a cloud,
His full un-bound-ed love.


---
Author: Mary Bowley Peters (1813-1856)
Tune: NearerHome
