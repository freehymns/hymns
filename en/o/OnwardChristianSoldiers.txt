Onward, Christian Soldiers

Verse 1:
On-ward, Chris-tian sol-diers, /:\n:/ March-ing as to war,
With the cross of Je-sus /:\n:/ Go-ing on be-fore.
Christ, the roy-al Mast-er, /:\n:/ Leads a-gainst the foe;
For-ward in-to bat-tle /:\n:/ See His ban-ners go!

Refrain:
On-ward, Chris-tian sol-diers, /:\n:/ March-ing as to war,
With the cross of Je-sus /:\n:/ Go-ing on be-fore.

Verse 2:
At the sign of tri-umph, /:\n:/ Sa-tan’s host doth flee;
On then, Chris-tian sol-diers, /:\n:/ On to vic-to-ry!
Hell’s foun-da-tions quiv-er /:\n:/ At the shout of praise;
Broth-ers, lift your voic-es, /:\n:/ Loud your an-thems raise.

Verse 3:
Like a might-y ar-my /:\n:/ Moves the Church of God;
Broth-ers, we are tread-ing /:\n:/ Where the saints have trod.
We are not di-vid-ed, /:\n:/ All one bod-y we,
One in hope, in doc-trine, /:\n:/ One in char-i-ty.

Verse 4:
Crowns and thrones may per-ish, /:\n:/ King-doms rise and wane,
But the Church of Je-sus /:\n:/ Con-stant will re-main.
Gates of hell can nev-er /:\n:/ ’Gainst that church pre-vail;
We have Christ’s own prom-ise, /:\n:/ And that can-not fail.

Verse 5:
On-ward then, ye peo-ple, /:\n:/ Join our hap-py throng,
Blend with ours your voic-es /:\n:/ In the tri-umph song.
Glo-ry, laud and hon-or Un-to /:\n:/ Christ the King,
This through count-less ag-es /:\n:/ Men and an-gels sing.


---
Author: Sabine Baring-Gould (1834–1924)
Tune: StGertrude
