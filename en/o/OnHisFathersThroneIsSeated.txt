On His Father'S Throne Is Seated

Verse 1:
On His Fa-ther's throne is seat-ed
Christ the Lord, the Liv-ing One!
All His toil on earth com-plet-ed,
All His work for sin-ners done.
In the glo-ry, in the glo-ry,
See Him, God's e-ter-nal Son!

Verse 2:
Ev-'ry knee shall bow be-fore Him,
Ev-'ry tongue con-fess His name,
Ran-somed myr-iads shall a-dore Him,
Who en-dured the sin-ners shame!
From the glo-ry, from the glo-ry,
God doth now His worth pro-claim.

Verse 3:
Man, the cross to Him a-ward-ed;
Man, the Sav-ior cru-ci-fied;
This world's judg-ment stands re-cord-ed,
God's own jus-tice sat-is-fied!
By the glo-ry, by the glo-ry,
Christ was claimed on earth who died.

Verse 4:
Son of Man, His in-car-na-tion
O-pened first the tale of grace;
Son of Man, in new cre-a-tion
Lead-er of a cho-sen race!
Well may glo-ry, Well may glo-ry,
Give to Him the hon-ored place!

Author: Thomas Kelly (1769-1854)
