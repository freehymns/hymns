On Earth The Song Begins

Verse 1:
On earth the song be-gins;
In heav'n more sweet and loud,
"To Him that cleansed our sins
By His a-ton-ing blood,
To Him," we sing in joy-ful strain,
"Be hon-or, pow'r, and praise, A-men.
Be hon-or, pow'r, and praise, A-men."

Verse 2:
A-lone He bore the cross,
A-lone its grief sus-tained;
His was the shame and loss,
And He the vic-t'ry gained;
The might-y work was all His own,
Though we shall share His glor-ious throne.
Though we shall share His glor-ious throne.


---
Author: Joseph Swain (1761-1796)
Tune: Lenox
