Once More Around Thy Table Lord

Verse 1:
Once more a-round Thy ta-ble, Lord,
And heed-ful of Thy par-ting word,
Our hearts from earth-ly care set free
Have, in Thy death, re-mem-bered Thee.

Verse 2:
Thy-self Thou gav-est, Son of God -
In love to us didst shed Thy blood;
Thy-self Thou gav-est to ful-fil
The Fa-ther's own e-ter-nal will.

Verse 3:
Thy-self Thou gav-est: may that word
Rest with us now in par-ting, Lord,
And lead us in de-vo-tion meet
To lay our lives at Thy blest feet.

Verse 4:
Thy-self Thou gav-est, Lord; and we
Would whol-ly give our-selves to Thee
In sim-ple faith and fer-vent love
To serve Thee 'till we meet a-bove.
