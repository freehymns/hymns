He Is Able To Deliver Thee

Verse 1:
’Tis the grand-est theme through the ag-es rung;
’Tis the grand-est theme for a mor-tal tongue;
’Tis the grand-est theme that the world e’er sung,
Our God is a-ble to de-liv-er thee.

Refrain:
He is a-ble to de-liv-er thee,
He is a-ble to de-liv-er thee;
Though by sin op-pressed, go to Him for rest;
Our God is a-ble to de-liv-er thee.

Verse 2:
’Tis the grand-est theme in the earth or main;
’Tis the grand-est theme for a mor-tal strain;
’Tis the grand-est theme, tell the world a-gain,
Our God is a-ble to de-liv-er thee.

Verse 3:
’Tis the grand-est theme, let the ti-dings roll,
To the guilt-y heart, to the sin-ful soul;
Look to God in faith, He will make thee whole,
Our God is a-ble to de-liv-er thee.


---
Author: William Augustine Ogden (1841–1897)
Tune: HeIsAbleToDeliverThee
