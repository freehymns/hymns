Hallelujah Joyful Raise

Verse 1:
Hal-le-lu-jah Joy-ful Raise
Heart and voice our God to praise!
Praise the Fa-ther! praise the Son!
Praise the Spir-it Three in One.

Verse 2:
One to per-fect all the plan
Of re-deem-ing ru-ined man!
Tri-une God to Thee be giv'n
Praise on earth and praise in heav'n.

---
Author: Newman Hall (1816-1902)
Tune: UniversityCollege
