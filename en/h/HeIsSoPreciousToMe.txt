He Is So Precious To Me

Verse 1:
So pre-cious is Je-sus, my Sav-ior, my king;
His praise all the day long with rap-ture I sing.
To Him in my weak-ness for strength I can cling,
For He is so pre-cious to me.

Refrain:
For He is so pre-cious, to me;
For He is so pre-cious, to me.
’Tis heav-en be-low,
My Re-deem-er to know,
For He is so pre-cious to me.

Verse 2:
He stood at my heart’s door ’mid sun-shine and rain,
And pa-tient-ly wait-ed an en-trance to gain.
What shame that so long He en-treat-ed in vain,
For He is so pre-cious to me.

Verse 3:
I stand on the moun-tain of bless-ing at last,
No cloud in the heav-ens a shad-ow to cast,
His smile is up-on me; the val-ley is past,
For He is so pre-cious to me.

Verse 4:
I praise Him be-cause He ap-point-ed a place
Where some day, through faith in His won-der-ful grace,
I know I shall see Him, shall look on His face,
For He is so pre-cious to me.


---
Author: Charles Hutchinson Gabriel (1856–1932)
Tune: HeIsSoPreciousToMe
