He Arose

Verse 1:
When the King a-wak-ened in His low-ly grave,
He a-rose; He a-rose.
He a-rose a vic-tor; He a-rose to save;
He a-rose; He a-rose.

Refrain:
He a-rose from the grave;
From its pow'r He will save.
He will save His peo-ple from the last of foes;
He a-rose; He a-rose.

Refrain:@e1
He a-rose; He a-rose from the grave;
From its pow'r He will save, will save.

He a-rose;

Verse 2:
There were an-gels watch-ing by His hum-ble bed;
He a-rose; He a-rose.
When He laid a-side the vest-ments of the dead,
He a-rose; He a-rose.

Verse 3:
Je-sus took a-way from death its aw-ful sting;
He a-rose; He a-rose.
In the dark, dark val-ley now His child can sing;
He a-rose; He a-rose.

Verse 4:
He a-rose, and now He reigns, a king on high;
He a-rose; He a-rose.
He a-rose a vic-tor, so by grace, shall I;
He a-rose; He a-rose.
