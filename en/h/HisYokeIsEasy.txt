His Yoke Is Easy

Verse 1:
The Lord is my shep-herd; I shall not want.
He mak-eth me down to lie
In pas-tures green He lead-eth me
The qui-et wa-ters by.

Refrain:
His yoke is eas-y; His bur-den is light.
I’ve found it so; I’ve found it so.
He lead-eth me by day and by night
Where liv-ing wa-ters flow.

Verse 2:
My soul cri-eth out: Re-store me a-gain,
And give me the strength to take
The nar-row path of right-eous-ness,
E’en for His own name’s sake.

Verse 3:
Yea, though I should walk the val-ley of death,
Yet why should I fear from ill?
For Thou art with me, and Thy rod
And staff me com-fort still.


---
Author: Ralph Erskine Hudson, 1885
