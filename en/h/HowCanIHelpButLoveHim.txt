How Can I Help But Love Him?

Verse 1:
Down from His splen-dor in glo-ry He came,
In-to a world of woe;
Took on Him-self all my guilt and my shame,
Why should He love me so?

Refrain:
How can I help but love Him,
When He loved me so?
How can I help but love Him,
When He loved me so?

Verse 2:
I am un-wor-thy to take of His grace,
Won-der-ful grace so free;
Yes, Je-sus suf-fered and died in my place,
E’en for a soul like me.

Verse 3:
He is the fair-est of thou-sands to me,
His love is sweet and true;
Won-der-ful beau-ty in Him I now see,
More than I ev-er knew.


---
Author: Elton Menno Roth, 1921
