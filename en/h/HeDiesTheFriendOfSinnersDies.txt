He Dies! The Friend of Sinners Dies

Verse 1:
He dies! the Friend of Sin-ners dies!
While Sa-lem’s daugh-ters weep a-round;
A so-lemn dark-ness veils the skies,
Then sud-den trem-bling shakes the ground.
Will you not drop a tear or two
As He is crushed be-neath the flood?
He shed a thou-sand drops for you:
A thou-sand drops of rich-est blood.

Verse 2:
Here’s love and grief be-yond de-gree:
The Lord of glo-ry dies for men!
But then what sud-den joys we see:
Je-sus who died, now lives a-gain.
In vain the powr's of hell as-sume,
That they can hold their high-est prize.
In vain the dark-ness of the tomb
For-bids the Son of God to rise.

Verse 3:
Break off your tears, you saints, and tell
How high your great de-liv'-rer reigns;
Sing how He spoiled the hosts of hell,
And led Death cap-tive in His chains.
Oh live for-ev-er, won-drous King!
Born to re-deem, and strong to save;
But where O Death is now thy sting?
And where’s thy vic-to-ry O Grave?
