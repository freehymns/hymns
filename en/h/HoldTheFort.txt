Hold the Fort

Verse 1:
Ho, my com-rades! see the sig-nal
Wav-ing in the sky!
Re-in-force-ments now ap-pear-ing,
Vic-to-ry is nigh.

Refrain:
Hold the fort, for I am com-ing,
Je-sus sig-nals still;
Wave the an-swer back to Heav-en,
By Thy grace we will.

Verse 2:
See the might-y host ad-vanc-ing,
Sa-tan lead-ing on;
Might-y ones a-round us fall-ing,
Cour-age al-most gone!

Verse 3:
See the glor-ious ban-ner wav-ing!
Hear the trump-et blow!
In our lead-er’s name we tri-umph
O-ver ev-’ry foe.

Verse 4:
Fierce and long the bat-tle rag-es,
But our help is near;
On-ward co-mes our great com-mand-er,
Cheer, my com-rad-es, cheer!


---
Author: Philip P. Bliss, 1870
Tune: HoldTheFort
