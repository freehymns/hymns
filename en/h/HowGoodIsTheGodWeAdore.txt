How Good Is The God We Adore

Verse 1:
How= good is the God we a-dore!
Our_ faith-ful, un-change-a-ble friend:
His_ love is as great as His pow'r
And= knows nei-ther meas-ure nor end.

Verse 2:
'Tis= Je-sus the first and the last;
Whose_ Spir-it will guide us safe home;
We'll_ praise Him for all that is past
And= trust Him for all that's to come.


---
Author: Joseph Hart (1712-1759)
Tune: Celeste
