He Did Not Come to Judge

Verse 1:
He did not come to judge the world,
He did not come to blame;
He did not on-ly come to seek,
It was to save He came;
And when we call Him Sa-vior,
And when we call Him Sa-vior,
And when we call Him Sa-vior then
We call Him by His name.

---
Topic: Christmas
Source: From Pilgrim Choruses (Pickering & Inglis, 1900)
