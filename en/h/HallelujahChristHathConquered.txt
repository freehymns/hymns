Hallelujah! Christ Hath Conquered

Verse 1:
Hal-le-lu-jah! Christ hath con-quered,
Con-quered sin and death and hell:
Sing a-loud His might-y tri-umphs,
Glad-ly now His prais-es tell;
Hal-le-lu-jah! Hal-le-lu-jah!
Je-sus hath done all things well.
Hal-le-lu-jah! Hal-le-lu-jah!
Je-sus hath done all things well.

Verse 2:
He who once as Man of sor-rows
Bore our load of sin and shame,
Glad we hail as King of glo-ry:
His a nev-er-end-ing fame;
Hal-le-lu-jah! Hal-le-lu-jah!
Praise, for ev-er praise, His name.
Hal-le-lu-jah! Hal-le-lu-jah!
Praise, for ev-er praise, His name.

Verse 3:
Son of God, in ad-o-ra-tion
Low we bow be-fore Thy face,
And with grate-ful hearts, Lord Je-sus,
Sing the mar-vels of Thy grace;
Hal-le-lu-jah! Hal-le-lu-jah!
Thee we wor-ship, Thee we praise
Hal-le-lu-jah! Hal-le-lu-jah!
Thee we wor-ship, Thee we praise
