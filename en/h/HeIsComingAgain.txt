He Is Coming Again

Verse 1:
Lift up your heads, pil-grims a-wea-ry,
See day’s ap-proach now crim-son the sky;
Night shad-ows flee, and your Be-lov-èd,
A-wait-ed with long-ing, at last draw-eth nigh.

Refrain:
He is com-ing a-gain, He is com-ing a-gain,
The ver-y same Je-sus, re-ject-ed of men;
He is com-ing a-gain, He is com-ing a-gain,
With pow'r and great glo-ry, He is com-ing a-gain!

Verse 2:
Dark was the night, sin warred a-gainst us;
Heav-y the load of sor-row we bore;
But now we see signs of His com-ing;
Our hearts glow with-in us, joy’s cup run-neth o’er!

Verse 3:
O bless-èd hope! O bliss-ful prom-ise!
Fill-ing our hearts with rap-ture di-vine;
O day of days! Hail Thy ap-pear-ing!
Thy tran-scend-ent glo-ry for-ev-er shall shine.

Verse 4:
E-ven so, come, pre-cious Lord Je-sus;
Cre-a-tion waits re-demp-tion to see;
Caught up in clouds, soon we shall meet Thee;
O bless-èd as-sur-ance, for-ev-er with Thee!

Author: Mabel Johnston Camp (1871–1937)
