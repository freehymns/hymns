By Faith I Look

Verse 1:
By faith I look where Christ has gone,
And see up-on His Fa-ther’s throne
A Man, with glo-ry crowned;
His brow is marred, and on His side,
Whence flowed the cleans-ing crim-son tide,
The marks of love are found.

Verse 2:
Here is the re-cord of the past,
Fruit of my sins that bound Him fast
To that de-grad-ing tree;
In ev'-ry wound I read my guilt,
And thank Him that His blood was spilt
To set my con-science free.

Verse 3:
I look a-gain, and now I see
That bless-èd Man en-gaged for me,
His hands up-lift-ed high;
Be-fore the throne of God He pleads,
God’s great High Priest, He in-ter-cedes,
And so pre-serves me nigh.

Verse 4:
Once more I gaze up-on that face,
And lo! as if to leave His place
He seems a-bout to rise;
Re-call-ing His “I quick-ly come”,
I learn His thought: to fetch me home,
To praise Him in the skies.

Verse 5:
What love! He washed my sins a-way,
Thus bold-ness in the Judg-ment Day
For me there doth re-main.
What grace! now oc-cu-pied with me,
He wills I should His glo-ry see,
When He re-turns a-gain!


---
Author: C. E. Peglar
Tune: Piety
