Bring a Torch

Verse 1:
Bring a torch, ~ Grace, Hope and Mer-cy,
Bring a torch, come swift-ly and run.
Christ is born, tell the folk of the vil-lage,
Je-sus is sleep-ing in His cra-dle,
Ah, ah, beau-ti-ful is the mo-ther,
Ah, ah, beau-ti-ful is her son.


Verse 2:
Ha-sten now, good folk of the vil-lage,
Ha-sten now, the Christ Child to see.
You will find Him a-sleep in a man-ger,
Qui-et-ly come and whis-per soft-ly,
Hush, hush, peace-ful-ly now He slum-bers,
Hush, hush, peace-ful-ly now He sleeps.

Verse 3:
Pon-der there, the birth of your Sav-ior,
Pon-der there, the mer-cy of God.
Though your sins be as dark as the mid-night
Lo He will of-fer you com-pass-ion,
Peace, peace, Je-sus will suf-fer for you.
Peace, peace, inn-o-cence bears the rod.
