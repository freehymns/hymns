Blest Be The Tie That Binds

Verse 1:
Blest be the tie that binds
Our hearts in Chris-tian love;
The fel-low-ship of kin-dred minds
Is like to that a-bove.

Verse 2:
Be-fore our Fa-ther’s throne
We pour our ar-dent pray'rs;
Our fears, our hopes, our aims are one
Our com-forts and our cares.

Verse 3:
We share each oth-er’s woes,
Our mut-ual bur-dens bear;
And of-ten for each oth-er flows
The sym-pa-thiz-ing tear.

Verse 4:
When we a-sun-der part,
It gives us in-ward pain;
But we shall still be joined in heart,
And hope to meet a-gain.

Verse 5:
This glor-ious hope re-vives
Our cour-age by the way;
While each in ex-pec-ta-tion lives,
And longs to see the day.

Verse 6:
From sor-row, toil and pain,
And sin, we shall be free,
And per-fect love and friend-ship reign
Through all e-ter-ni-ty.


---
Author: John Fawcett, 1782
Tune: Dennis
