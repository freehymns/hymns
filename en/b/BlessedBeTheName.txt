Blessèd Be the Name

Verse 1:
All praise to Him who reigns a-bove
In maj-es-ty su-preme,
Who gave His Son for man to die,
That He might man re-deem!

Refrain:
Bless-èd be the name!
Bless-èd be the name!
Bless-èd be the name of the Lord!
Bless-èd be the name!
Bless-èd be the name!
Bless-èd be the name of the Lord!

Verse 2:
His name a-bove all names shall stand,
Ex-alt-ed more and more,
At God the Fa-ther’s own right hand,
Where an-gel hosts a-dore.

Verse 3:
Re-deem-er, Sav-ior, friend of man
Once ru-ined by the fall,
Thou hast de-vised sal-va-tion’s plan,
For Thou hast died for all.

Verse 4:
His name shall be the coun-sel-or,
The might-y Prince of Peace,
Of all earth’s king-doms con-quer-or,
Whose reign shall nev-er cease.


---
Author: William H. Clark (1854–1925)
Author, Refrain: Ralph Hudson (1843–1901)
