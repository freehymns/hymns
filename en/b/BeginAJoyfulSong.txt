Begin a Joyful Song

Verse 1:
Be-gin a joy-ful song,
Sing of this hap-py morn:
Glad tid-ings from an an-gel’s tongue,
"This day is Je-sus born!"

Verse 2:
What com-forts does His name
To sin-ful men af-ford!
His glor-ious ti-tles we pro-claim
A Sav-ior: Christ the Lord!

Verse 3:
Now wrapped in swad-ling bands,
See the e-ter-nal Son!
A sta-ble for His pal-ace stands,
A man-ger for His throne!

Verse 4:
Glo-ry to God on high,
For this most hap-py morn!
Come join the an-thems of the sky
"This day is Je-sus born!"

---
Topic: Christmas
Tune: FestalSong
Author: John Cawood (1819)
