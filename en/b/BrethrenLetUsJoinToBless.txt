Brethren, Let Us Join To Bless

Verse 1:
Breth-ren, let us join to bless
Je-sus Christ, our joy and peace
He, who bowed His head so low
Un-der-neath our load of woe.

Verse 2:
His the curse, the wounds, the gall,
His the stripes: He bore them all;
His the dy-ing and the pain
When our sins He did sus-tain.

Verse 3:
He, th'ac-cept-ed Sac-ri-fice,
From the van-quished grave did rise;
Free Him-self, He set us free
In His per-fect lib-er-ty.

Verse 4:
Ran-somed now, ac-cept-ed, free,
Safe from judg-ment, Lord, in Thee,
We re-joice that God can bless
All who do Thy name con-fess.

Verse 5:
Praise our God who willed it thus;
Praise the Lamb who died for us;
Praise the Fa-ther, through the Son,
Who so vast a work hath done!


---
Author: John Cennick (1718-1755)
Tune: Innocents
