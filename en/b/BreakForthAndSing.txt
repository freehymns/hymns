Break Forth and Sing

Verse 1:
Break forth and sing the song
Of Glo-ry to the Lamb!
Wake ev-'ry heart and ev-'ry tongue,
To praise the Sav-ior's name!

Verse 2:
Sing of His dy-ing love;
Sing of His ris-ing pow'r;
Sing how He in-ter-cedes a-bove
For those whose sins He bore.

Verse 3:
Sing on your heav'n-ly road;
Ye sons of glo-ry, sing;
To the as-cend-ed Lamb of God,
Your cheer-ful prais-es bring.

Verse 4:
Soon shall we hear Him say,
Ye ran-somed pil-grims, come;
Soon will He call us hence a-way,
And take us to His home.

Verse 5:
Then shall each rap-tured tongue,
His full-est praise pro-claim;
And sweet-er voic-es wake the song
Of Glo-ry to the Lamb!

---
Author: Isaac Watts
Tune: StThomas
