Baptismal Hymn

Verse 1:
A-round Thy grave, Lord Je-sus,
In spir-it here we stand,
With hearts all full of prais-es,
To keep Thy blest com-mand.
By faith, our souls re-joic-ing,
To trace Thy path of love,
Through death's dark an-gry bil-lows,
Up to the throne a-bove.

Verse 2:
Lord, Je-sus, we re-mem-ber
The trav-ail of Thy soul,
When in Thy love's deep pit-y
The waves did o’er Thee roll.
Bapt-ized in death's cold wat-ers,
For us Thy blood was shed,
For us Thou, Lord of Glor-y,
Wast num-bered with the dead.

Verse 3:
O Lord, Thou now art ris-en,
Thy tra-vail all is o'er;
For sin Thou once has suf-fered:
Thou liv'st to die no more.
Sin, death, and hell are van-quished
By Thee, the Church-'s Head;
And now we share Thy tri-umph,
Thou First-born from the dead.

Verse 4:
Un-to Thy death bapt-iz-ed,
We own with Thee we died;
With Thee, our life, we're ris-en
And shall be glor-i-fied.
From sin, the world, and Sa-tan,
We're ran-somed by Thy blood,
And here would walk as stran-gers,
A-live with Thee to God.
