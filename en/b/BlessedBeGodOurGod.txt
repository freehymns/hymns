Blessed Be God, Our God

Verse 1:
Bless-èd be God, our God,
Who gave for us His well-be-lov-èd Son,
The gift of gifts, all oth-er gifts in one;
Bless-èd be God, our God!

Verse 2:
What will He not be-stow!
Who free-ly gave this might-y gift un-bought,
Un-mer-it-ed, un-heed-ed, and un-sought,
What will He not be-stow?

Verse 3:
He spar-èd not His Son!
'Tis this that si-lenc-es each ris-ing fear,
'Tis this that bids the hard thought dis-ap-pear;
He spar-èd not His Son!

Verse 4:
Who shall con-demn us now?
Since Christ has died, and ris'n, and gone a-bove,
For us to plead at the right hand of Love,
Who shall con-demn us now?

Verse 5:
'Tis God that jus-ti-fies!
Who shall re-call His par-don or His grace?
Or who the bro-ken chain of guilt re-place?
'Tis God that jus-ti-fies!

Verse 6:
The vic-to-ry is ours!
For us in might came forth the might-y One;
For us He fought the fight, the tri-umph won:
The vic-to-ry is ours!


---
Author: Horatius Bonar (1808-1889)
Tune: HerefordNew
