Balm in Gilead

Refrain:
There is a balm in Gilead
To make the wound-ed whole;
There is a balm in Gilead
To heal the sin sick soul.

Verse 1:
Some times I feel dis-cour-aged,
And think my work’s in vain,
But then the Ho-ly Spir-it
Re-vives my soul a-gain.

Verse 2:
If you can’t preach like Pe-ter,
If you can’t pray like Paul,
Just tell the love of Je-sus,
And say He died for all.
