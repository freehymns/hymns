Behold The Throne Of Grace

Verse 1:
Be-hold the throne of grace!
The prom-ise calls us near!
To seek our God and Fa-ther's face,
Who loves to an-swer prayer.

Verse 2:
That rich a-ton-ing blood,
Which sprin-kled 'round we see,
Pro-vides for those who come to God
An all-pre-vail-ing plea.

Verse 3:
My soul, ask what thou wilt,
Thou canst not be too bold:
Since His own blood for thee was spilt,
What else can He with-hold?

Verse 4:
Be-yond our ut-most wants
His love and pow'r can bless:
To pray-ing souls He al-ways grants
More than they can ex-press.

Verse 5:
Since 'tis the Lord's com-mand,
Our mouth we'll o-pen wide:
Lord, o-pen Thou Thy boun-teous hand,
That we may be sup-plied.

Verse 6:
Thine im-age Lord, be-stow,
Thy pres-ence and Thy love:
We ask to serve Thee here be-low,
And reign with Thee a-bove.


---
Author: John Newton (1725-1807)
Tune: StMichael
