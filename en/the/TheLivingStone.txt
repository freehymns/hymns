The Living Stone

Verse 1:
On Christ, sal-va-tion rests se-cure;
The Rock of Ag-es must en-dure;
Nor can that faith be o-ver-thrown
Which rests up-on the “Liv-ing Stone.”

Verse 2:
No oth-er hope shall in-ter-vene;
To Him we look, on Him we lean;
Oth-er foun-da-tions we dis-own,
And build on Christ, the “Liv-ing Stone.”

Verse 3:
In Him it is or-dained to raise
A tem-ple to Je-ho-vah’s praise,
Com-posed of all His saints, who own
No Sav-ior but the “Liv-ing Stone.”

Verse 4:
View the vast build-ing, see it rise:
The work how great! the plan how wise!
O won-drous fab-ric! pow’r un-known:
That rests it on the “Liv-ing Stone.”

Verse 5:
But most a-dore His pre-cious name:
His glo-ry and His grace pro-claim!
For us, the lost, con-demned, un-done,
He gave Him-self, the “Liv-ing Stone.”


---
Author: Samuel Medley (1738-1799)
Tune: DukeStreet
