The Holy One Who Knew No Sin

Verse 1:
The Ho-ly One who knew no sin,
God made Him sin for us;
The Sav-ior died our souls to win,
Up-on the shame-ful cross.
His pre-cious blood a-lone a-vailed
To wash our sins a-way;
Through weak-ness He o'er hell pre-vailed,
Through death He won the day.

Verse 2:
His beau-ty shin-eth far a-bove
Our fee-ble pow'r of praise,
And we shall live and learn His love
Through ev-er-last-ing days.
The know-ing this, that us He loves,
Hath made our cup run o'er;
Je-sus, Thy name our spir-it moves,
To-day and ev-er-more.


---
Author: Hannah K. Burlingham (1842-1901)
Tune: DrinkToMeOnly
