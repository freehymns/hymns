The Person Of The Christ

Verse 1:
The per-son of the Christ,
En-fold-ing ev-'ry grace,
Once slain, but now a-live a-gain,
In heav'n de-mands our praise.

Verse 2:
With joy of Him we sing,
Since we with Him are dead:
Our life is hid with Christ in God,
In Christ the Church-'s Head.

Verse 3:
The heav'ns are o-pened now!
Pro-claim through earth a-broad;
And we, by faith, in heav'n be-hold
Our Sav-ior, Christ, the Lord!


---
Author: Miss F. T. Wigram
Tune: Boylston
