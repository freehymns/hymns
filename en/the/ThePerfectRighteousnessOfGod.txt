The Perfect Righteousness Of God

Verse 1:
The per-fect right-eous-ness of God
Is wit-nessed in the Sav-ior's blood;
'Tis in the cross of Christ we trace
His right-eous-ness, yet won-drous grace.

Verse 2:
God could not pass the sin-ner by;
Jus-tice de-mands that he should die;
But in the cross of Christ we see
How God can save, yet right-eous be.

Verse 3:
The judg-ment fell on Je-sus' head
'Twas in His blood sin's debt was paid;
Stern Jus-tice can de-mand no more,
And Mer-cy can dis-pense her store.

Verse 4:
The sin-ner who be-lieves is free,
Can say, "The Sav-ior died for me,"
Can point to the a-ton-ing blood
And say, "This made my peace with God."


---
Author: Albert Midlane (1825-1909)
Tune: Mendon
