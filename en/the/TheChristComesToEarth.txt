The Christ Comes to Earth

Verse 1:
The mer-ry bells are gai-ly ring-ing,
Hear their joy-ous chime;
They wel-come in the Hope of na-tions
Born this Christ-mas time;
While choirs of an-gels sing ex-ult-ing,
Let our voic-es rise
In notes of praise to the e-ter-nal
Lord of earth and skies.

Refrain:
Hail! all hail! Bless-èd birth;
The blest de-sire of ev-ery na-tion
Comes at last to earth;
Hail! all hail! Christ your king,
The ho-ly One, the great Re-deem-er,
Life and light doth bring.

Verse 2:
Re-peat to all the won-drous tid-ings,
Sweet-est ev-er heard;
The Shi-loh com-eth to His tem-ple,
God re-deems His word;
In hu-man form He now des-cend-eth,
My-ster-y of love;
O hail the source of ev-ery bless-ing,
Throned in light a-bove.

Verse 3:
A-wake! a-wake! ye slum-b'ring na-tions,
Christ is at your door;
The roy-al Babe of Beth-l’hem’s man-ger,
Praise Him ev-er-more;
All glo-ry in the high-est sing-ing,
Now His pow'r con-fess;
Crown Him the Prince of Peace tri-um-phant,
Lord of Right-eous-ness.

Topic: Christmas
Author: Lizzie DeArmond (1907)
