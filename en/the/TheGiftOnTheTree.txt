The Gift on the Tree

Verse 1:
Christ-mas be-gan when Christ be-came man
At Beth-le-hem’s man-ger one day,
But it was not done Till God gave His Son
To take our trans-gres-sions a-way.
And I get a thrill on Cal-va-ry’s hill
Re-cal-ling what Christ did to save me,
En-joy-ing the love that came from a-bove
When God hung His Gift on the Tree.

---
Topic: Christmas
Author: Bud Moris (2001)
