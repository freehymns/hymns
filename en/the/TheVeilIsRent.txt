The Veil Is Rent

Verse 1:
The veil is rent: Lo! Je-sus stands
Be-fore the throne of grace;
And clouds of in-cense from his hands
Fill all that glor-ious place.

Verse 2:
His pre-cious blood is sprin-kled there,
Be-fore and on the throne;
And His own wounds in heav'n de-clare
The work that saves is done.

Verse 3:
"Tis fin-ished!" on the cross He said,
In ag-o-nies and blood;
'Tis fin-ished! now He lives to plead
Be-fore the face of God.

Verse 4:
'Tis fin-ished! here our souls can rest,
His work can nev-er fail;
By Him our Sac-ri-fice and Priest,
We en-ter through the veil.

Verse 5:
With-in the ho-li-est of all,
Cleansed by His pre-cious blood,
Be-fore the throne we pros-trate fall,
And wor-ship Thee, our God.

Verse 6:
Bold-ly our heart and voice we raise,
His name, His blood, our plea;
As-sured our pray'rs and songs of praise
As-cend, by Him, to Thee.


---
Author: James G. Deck (1807-1884)
Tune: StPeter
