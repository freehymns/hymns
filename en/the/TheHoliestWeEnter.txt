The Holiest We Enter

Verse 1:
The ho-li-est we en-ter
In per-fect peace with God,
Through whom we found our cen-ter
In Je-sus and His blood:
Though great may be our dull-ness
In thought and word and deed,
We glo-ry in the full-ness
Of Him that meets our need.

Verse 2:
Much in-cense is as-cend-ing
Be-fore th'e-ter-nal throne;
God gra-cious-ly is bend-ing
To hear each fee-ble groan;
To all our prayers and prais-es
Christ adds His sweet per-fume,
And love the cen-ser rais-es,
These o-dors to con-sume.

Verse 3:
O God, we come with sing-ing,
Be-cause the great High Priest
Our names to Thee is bring-ing,
Nor e'er for-gets the least:
For us He wears the mi-tre,
Where ho-li-ness shines bright,
For us His rob-es are whiter
Than heav'n's un-sul-lied light.
