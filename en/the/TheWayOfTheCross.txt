The Way Of The Cross

Verse 1:
I must needs go home by the way of the cross,
There’s no oth-er way but this;
I shall ne’er get sight of the gates of light,
If the way of the cross I miss.

Refrain:
The way of the cross leads home,
The way of the cross leads home,
It is sweet to know as I on-ward go,
The way of the cross leads home.

Verse 2:
I must needs go on in the blood sprin-kled way,
The path that the Sav-ior trod,
If I ev-er climb to the heights sub-lime,
Where the soul is at home with God.

Verse 3:
Then I bid fare-well to the way of the world,
To walk in it nev-er more;
For the Lord says, "Come," and I seek my home,
Where He waits at the o-pen door.
