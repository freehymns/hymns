The Cross! the Cross!

Verse 1:
The cross! the cross! the blood-stained Cross!
The cross of Christ I see!
It tells me of that pre-cious blood
That once was shed for me.

Verse 2:
That wrath! the wrath! the aw-ful wrath
That Je-sus felt for me,
When bear-ing my sin's heav-y load
He died on Cal-va-ry.

Verse 3:
But Je-sus lives! the Sav-ior lives!
In heav'n He pleads for me;
And bold-ly I ap-proach to God,
His blood my on-ly plea.

Verse 4:
He comes! He comes! the Sav-ior comes!
Who bled and died for me;
Then will I sing, with rapt-ure sing,
When gaz-ing, Lord, on Thee.


---
Author, verse 1: John H. Stockton
Tune: TheHallowedCrossNoRefrain
