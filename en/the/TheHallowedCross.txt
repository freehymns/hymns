The Hallowed Cross

Verse 1:
The Cross! the Cross! that blood-stained Cross!
The hal-lowed Cross I see!
Re-mind-ing me of pre-cious blood
That once was shed for me.

Refrain:
Oh, the blood! the pre-cious blood!
That Je-sus shed for me,
Up-on the Cross in crim-son flood,
Just now by faith I see.

Verse 2:
That Cross! that Cross! that heav-y Cross,
My Sav-ior bore for me,
Which bowed Him to the earth with grief,
On sad Mount Cal-va-ry.

Verse 3:
The crown! the crown! the glor-ious crown!
The crown of vic-to-ry!
The crown of life! It shall be mine
When I shall Je-sus see.

Verse 4:
My tears, un-bid-den, seem to flow,
For love, un-bound-ed love,
Which guides me thro’ this world of woe
And points to joys a-bove.


---
Author: John H. Stockton
Tune: TheHallowedCross
