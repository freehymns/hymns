The Savior With Me

Verse 1:
I must have the Sav-ior with me,
For I dare not go a-lone,
I must feel His pres-ence near me,
And His arm a-round me thrown.

Refrain:
Then my soul shall fear no ill,
Let Him lead me where He will,
I will go with-out a mur-mur,
And His foot-steps fol-low still.

Refrain:@e1
Then my soul shall fear no ill, fear no ill,
Let Him lead me where He will, where He will,
I will go

Verse 2:
I must have the Sav-ior with me,
For my faith, at best, is weak;
He can whis-per words of com-fort,
That no oth-er voice can speak.

Verse 3:
I must have the Sav-ior with me,
In the on-ward march of life,
Through the tem-pest and the sun-shine,
Through the bat-tle and the strife.

Verse 4:
I must have the Sav-ior with me,
And His eye the way must guide,
Till I reach the vale of Jor-dan,
Till I cross the roll-ing tide.


---
Author: Fanny Crosby, 1884
