Merry Christmas from Japan

Verse 1:@B1
Low-ly shep-herds of Ju-de-a,
On a win-ter’s night,
Heard a sound of an-gel voic-es,
Saw a won-drous light.

Refrain:@G1
O, Cel-e-brate the birth of the in-fant Ye-su!_
We wish you a ve-ry mer-ry Ku-ri-su-ma-su!

Verse 2:@B1
East-ern wise men seek-ing Je-sus,
Trav-’ling from a-far,
Just like us were filled with glad-ness,
When they saw His star.

Verse 3:@B1
Sim-eon blessed Him in the temp-le,
An-na wor-shiped too,
Glad that she had seen her Lord at
Ku-ri-su-ma-su!

Verse 4:@B1
Friends to-day are keep-ing Christ-mas
In A-mer-i-ca,
Bro-thers, sis-ters, sing-ing with us,
Down in Af-ri-ca.
