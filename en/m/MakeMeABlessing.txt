Make Me A Blessing

Verse 1:
Out in the high-ways and by-ways of life,
Ma-ny are wea-ry and sad;
Car-ry the sun-shine where dark-ness is rife,
Mak-ing the sor-row-ing glad.

Refrain:
Make me a bless-ing,
Make me a bless-ing,
Out of my life may Je-sus shine;
Make me a bless-ing, O Sav-ior, I pray,
Make me a bless-ing to some-one to-day.

Verse 2:
Tell the sweet sto-ry of Christ and His love,
Tell of His pow’r to for-give;
Oth-ers will trust Him if on-ly you prove
True, ev-'ry mo-ment you live.

Verse 3:
Give as ’twas giv-en to you in your need,
Love as the Mast-er loved you;
Be to the help-less a help-er in-deed,
Un-to your mis-sion be true.
