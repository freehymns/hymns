My Shepherd Is The Lamb

Verse 1:
My shep-herd is the Lamb,
The liv-ing Lord who died;
With all things good I ev-er am
By Him sup-plied.
He rich-ly feeds my soul
With bless-ings from a-bove,
And leads me where the riv-ers roll
Of end-less love.

Verse 2:
My soul He doth re-store
When-e’er I go a-stray;
He makes my cup of joy run o’er
From day to day;
His love, so full, so free,
A-noints my head with oil;
Mer-cy and good-ness fol-low me,
Fruit of His toil.

Verse 3:
When faith and hope shall cease,
And love a-bide a-lone,
Then shall I see Him face to face,
And know as known.
Still shall I lift my voice,
His praise my song shall be;
And I will in His love re-joice
Who died for me.


---
Author: John Beaumont (1762–1822)
Tune: Priory
Variant: Our Shepherd is the Lord
