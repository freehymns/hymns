Must Jesus Bear The Cross Alone

Verse 1:
Must Je-sus bear the cross a-lone,
And all the world go free?
No, there’s a cross for ev-'ry-one,
And there’s a cross for me.

Verse 2:
The con-se-crat-ed cross I’ll bear
Till death shall set me free;
And then go home my crown to wear,
Yes, there’s a crown for me.

Verse 3:
O pre-cious cross! O glor-ious crown!
O res-ur-rec-tion day!
I long for when the Lord comes down
And bears my soul a-way.


---
Author: Thomas Shepherd, 1693
Author, verse 2: George Allen, 1844
Tune: Maitland
