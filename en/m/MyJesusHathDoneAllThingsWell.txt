My Jesus Hath Done All Things Well

Verse 1:
Now, in a song of grate-ful praise,
To my dear Lord my voice I’ll raise;
With all His saints I’ll join to tell:
My Je-sus hath done all things well.

Verse 2:
All worlds His glor-ious pow'r con-fess,
His wis-dom all His works ex-press;
But oh! His love what tongue can tell?
My Je-sus hath done all things well.

Verse 3:
How sov-'reign, pow-er-ful and free
Hath been His love to sin-ful me!
He plucked me from the jaws of hell:
My Je-sus hath done all things well.

Verse 4:
I spurned His grace, I broke His laws,
And then he un-der-took my cause;
To save me though I did reb-el,
My Je-sus hath done all things well.

Verse 5:
And since my soul hath known His love,
What bless-ings hath he made me prove!
Mer-cy which doth all praise ex-cel,
My Je-sus hath done all things well.

Verse 6:
When-e’er my Sav-ior or my God,
Hath on me laid His gen-tle rod;
I know in all that hath be-fell,
That Je-sus hath done all things well.

Verse 7:
Though man-y~a flam-ing fier-y dart,
At-tempt their lev-el at my heart;
With this I all his rage re-pel:
My Je-sus hath done all things well.

Verse 8:
Some-times the Lord His face doth hide,
To make me pray, and kill my pride;
Yet on my heart it still doth dwell,
That Je-sus hath done all things well.

Verse 9:
Soon I shall pass this vale of death,
And in His arms re-sign my breath:
Yet then my hap-py soul shall tell,
How Je-sus hath done all things well.

Verse 10:
When to bright re-gions I shall rise,
And join the an-them of the skies;
This grate-ful note shall ev-er swell,
My Je-sus hath done all things well.
