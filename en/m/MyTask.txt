My Task

Verse 1:
To love some-one more dear-ly ev-'ry day,
To help a wan-d'ring child to find his way,
To pon-der o’er a no-ble thought and pray,
And smile when eve-ning falls,
And smile when eve-ning falls:
This is my task.

Verse 2:
To fol-low truth as blind men long for light,
To do my best from dawn of day till night,
To keep my heart fit for His ho-ly sight,
And an-swer when He calls,
And an-swer when He calls:
This is my task.

Verse 3:
And then my Sav-ior by and by to meet,
When faith hath made my task on earth com-plete,
And lay my hom-age at the Mast-er’s feet,
With-in the jas-per walls,
With-in the jas-per walls:
This crowns my task.


---
Author: Emma Louise Hindle Ashford, 1902
Tune: MyTask
