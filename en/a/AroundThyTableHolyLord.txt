Around Thy Table, Holy Lord

Verse 1:
A-round Thy ta-ble, ho-ly Lord,
In fel-low-ship we meet,
O-be-di-ent to Thy gra-cious word,
This feast of love to eat.

Verse 2:
Here ev'-ry one that loves Thy name,
Our will-ing hearts em-brace;
Our source of life and hope the same,
All debt-ors to Thy grace.

Verse 3:
Com-mune with each at this blest hour,
Thy peace, Thy joy im-part;
Thy thoughts of love, of truth, of pow'r,
Im-press up-on each heart.


---
Author: Mary Bowley Peters (1813-1856)
Tune: Evan
