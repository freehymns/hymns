All Glory Be to God

Verse 1:
All glo-ry be to God on high,
And peace to earth des-cend;
God leaves His throne a-bove the sky
And shows Him-self our friend!
The once in-vi-si-ble ap-pears,
The blest, the great I AM,
He so-journs in this vale of tears,
And Je-sus is His name.

Verse 2:
He's by the an-gels all a-dored,
Their mak-er and their king;
And tid-ings of their hum-bled Lord
They now to mor-tals bring;
God, emp-tied of His ma-jes-ty,
Of daz-zling glo-ries shorn,
Our be-ing’s source be-gins to be,
For God Him-self is born!

Verse 3:
See the e-ter-nal Son of God
A mor-tal Son of Man,
Now dwell-ing in an earth-ly clod
Whom Heav'n can-not con-tain!
Stand awed, oh hea-vens, look at this!
The Lord of earth and skies
Lo, hum-bled to the dust He is,
And in a man-ger lies!

Verse 4:
Now we the sons of men re-joice
Our Prince of Peace pro-claim,
With Hea-ven’s host lift up your voice,
And shout Im-man-uel’s name;
Our knees and hearts to Him we bow,
He's of our flesh and bone;
Yes Je-sus is our bro-ther now,
And God is all our own!

---

Edits: Edited for archaic language more even meter
Topic: Christmas
