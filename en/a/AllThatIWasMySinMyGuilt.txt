All That I Was

Verse 1:
All that I was, my sin, my guilt,
My death, was all mine own;
All that I am I owe to Thee,
My gra-cious God, a-lone.
My gra-cious God, a-lone.

Verse 2:
The e-vil of my form-er state
Was mine, and on-ly mine;
The good in which I now re-joice
Is Thine, and on-ly Thine.
Is Thine, and on-ly Thine.

Verse 3:
The dark-ness of my form-er state,
The bond-age, all was mine;
The light of life in which I walk,
The lib-er-ty, is Thine.
The lib-er-ty, is Thine.

Verse 4:
Thy Word first made me feel my sin,
It taught me to be-lieve;
Then, in be-liev-ing, peace I found,
And now I live, I live!
And now I live, I live!

Verse 5:
All that I am, e’en here on earth
All that I hope to be,
When Je-sus comes and glo-ry dawns,
I owe it, Lord, to Thee.
I owe it, Lord, to Thee.


---
Author: Horatius Bonar (1808–1889)
Tune: Ortonville
