Accepted In the Beloved

Verse 1:
"In the Be-lov-èd" ac-cept-ed am I,
Ris-en, as-cend-ed, and seat-ed on high;
Saved from all sin thro’ His in-fi-nite grace,
With the re-deemed ones ac-cord-ed a place.

Refrain:
"In the Be-lov-èd", God’s mar-vel-ous grace
Calls me to dwell in this won-der-ful place;
God sees my Sav-ior, and then He sees me,
"In the Be-lov-èd", ac-cept-ed and free.

Verse 2:
"In the Be-lov-èd" how safe my re-treat,
"In the Be-lov-èd" ac-count-ed com-plete;
Who can con-demn me? In Him I am free,
Sav-ior and Keep-er for-ev-er is He.

Verse 3:
"In the Be-lov-èd" I went to the tree,
There, in His Per-son, by faith I may see
In-fi-nite wrath roll-ing o-ver His head,
In-fi-nite grace, for He died in my stead.


---
Author: Civilla D. Martin, 1930
