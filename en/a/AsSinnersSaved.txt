As Sinners Saved

Verse 1:
As sin-ners saved we glad-ly praise
The Au-thor of re-deem-ing grace;
Fa-ther, 'tis Thine al-might-y pow'r
Se-cures us when the tem-pests low'r.

Verse 2:
Thy love's a ref-uge ev-er nigh,
Thy watch-ful-ness a moun-tain high,
Thy name a rock, which winds a-bove
And waves be-low can nev-er move.

Verse 3:
Thy faith-ful-ness, for ev-er sure,
Through end-less age-s shall en-dure;
Thy per-fect work shall ev-er prove
The depth of Thine un-ceas-ing love.

Verse 4:
While all things change, Thou chang-est not,
For-get-ting ne'er, though oft for-got;
Thy love, im-mu-ta-bly the same,
Dis-plays the glo-ry of Thy Name.

Verse 5:
Lord, we would then re-joice and praise
The Source of all this wond-rous grace;
Fa-ther, Thine ev-er-last-ing pow'r
Will keep us safe in dan-ger's hour.

---
Author: James H. Evans (1785-1849)
Tune: DukeStreet
