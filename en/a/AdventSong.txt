Advent Song

Verse 1:
Hear the ad-vent song Of the an-gel throng
As they ho-vered ov-er earth;
As the night shone bright with ce-les-tial light,
As they told of Je-sus' birth.

Refrain:
Glo=ry to God, in the high=est!
Peace on earth, good will to men.
May that song al-ways fill our hearts with praise,
Till the Lord shall come a-gain!

Verse 2:
Glo-ry be to God! All His works we laud!
But of all His gifts, the best
Is the Son he gave, sin-ful men to save;
Be His name for-ev-er blest.

Verse 3:
We will seek His face, We will know His grace,
We will wor-ship at His shrine;
All our trea-sure lay at His feet to-day,
As we hail Him king di-vine!

Verse 4:
At His cra-dle bend, For in Him des-cend
All the bless-ings God can give;
At His shame-ful cross, count your gain as loss,
By His death we sin-ners live.

Verse 5:
Peace on earth shall reign When He comes a-gain,
Lord of lords, and King of kings;
E-ven now God's rest fills the trou-bled breast,
When the Lord His pre-sence brings.

Verse 6:
Let the sons of light, Through the world's dark night,
As the watch-ers wait the dawn,
Look with eag-er eyes for the new sun-rise
Which shall bring the end-less morn!

---
Author: Arthur T. Pierson (1897)
Terms: Public Domain
Topic: Christmas
Tune: BigBen
Edits: The refrain has been altered.
