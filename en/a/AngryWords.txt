Angry Words

Verse 1:
An-gry words! O let them nev-er,
From the tongue un-bri-dled slip,
May the heart’s best im-pulse ev-er,
Check them ere they soil the lip.

Love is much too pure and ho-ly,
Friend-ship is too sa-cred far,
