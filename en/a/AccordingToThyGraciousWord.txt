According To Thy Gracious Word

Verse 1:
Ac-cord-ing to Thy gra-cious word,
In deep hu-mil-i-ty
This would I do, O Christ, my Lord,
I would re-mem-ber Thee.

Verse 2:
The bod-y, giv-en for my sake,
My bread from heav'n shall be:
Thy tes-ta-men-tal cup I take,
And thus re-mem-ber Thee.

Verse 3:
Re-mem-ber Thee, and all Thy pains,
And all Thy love to me;
Yea, while a breath, a pulse re-mains,
Would I re-mem-ber Thee.

Verse 4:
And when, O Lord, Thou com'st a-gain,
And I Thy glo-ry see,
For-ev-er as the Lamb once slain,
I will re-mem-ber Thee.


---
Author: James Montgomery (1791-1854)
Tune: StAgnes
