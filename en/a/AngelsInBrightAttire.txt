Angels In Bright Attire

Verse 1:
An-gels in bright at-tire
Des-cend from Heav’n to earth,
And spread the joy-ful news
Of our Re-deem-er’s birth.
See in a shed: a cat-tle stall
The Mas-ter and the Lord of all.

Verse 2:
"Glo-ry to God!" they sing
In most ex-alt-ed strains,
"Peace and good will to men
So long as Je-sus reigns."
And reign He shall, from shore to shore
Till sun and moon shall shine no more.

Verse 3:
We’ll join the heav’n-ly song,
And sound His name a-broad;
"Wel-come, the won-drous Man,
The great in-car-nate God."
O source di-vine of life and joy,
Thy prais-es shall our lips em-ploy.

Verse 4:
For love so great as Thine,
Our-selves to Thee we give,
And seek to do Thy will
As long as we shall live.
Be-fore Thy feet we prost-rate fall
And own Thee as the Lord of all.

---
Topic: Christmas
Tune: Darwalls148th
Author: Benjamin Beddome (1717-1795)
