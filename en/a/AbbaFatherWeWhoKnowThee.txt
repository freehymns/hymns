Abba, Father, We Who Know Thee

Verse 1:
Ab-ba, Fa-ther, we who know Thee
Gath-er ‘round the throne of grace:
Here we see Thy love and glo-ry
Shin-ing in the Sav-ior’s face!

Verse 2:
Weak-ness, fail-ure, all con-fess-ing,
We Thy prom-ised suc-cor claim,
Plead-ing for the need-ed bless-ing,
Je-sus’ ev-er-pre-cious name!

Verse 3:
Guide, in prayer, our needs ex-press-ing
In sweet fil-ial lib-er-ty,
Whilst with grate-ful hearts ad-dress-ing
Wor-ship, praise and thanks to Thee!

Verse 4:
Ev-’ry care up-on Thee cast-ing,
We would rest in love di-vine,
‘Till, in glo-ry ev-er-last-ing,
In Thy heav’n-ly light we shine!


---
Author: G. W. Frazer
Tune: Dijon
