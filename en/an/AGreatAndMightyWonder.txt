A Great and Mighty Wonder

Verse 1:
A great and migh-ty won-der,
A glor-ious mys-ter-y:
A vir-gin bears an inf-ant
Who veils His de-it-y.

Refrain:
Sing “glor-i-a” a-gain:
“To God on high be glo-ry
And peace on earth to men.”

Verse 2:
The Word be-comes in-car-nate
Yet still He reigns on high,
And cher-u-bim sing an-thems
To shep-herds from the sky.

Verse 3:
While thus they praise your mon-arch,
You hosts of heav'n-ly bands,
Re-joice O vales and mount-ains,
And o-ceans, clap your hands.

Verse 4:
Since all He comes to ran-som,
By all be He a-dored,
The in-fant born of Dav-id,
The Sav-ior and the Lord.

Verse 5:
All i-dols then shall per-ish
And strife and err-or cease,
And Christ shall raise his scep-ter,
Then shall be end-less peace.

Topic: Christmas
Original Language: Greek
Author: St. Germanus (734)
Translator: John M. Neale (1862)
Edits: Edited
