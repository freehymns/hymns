Once More, Before We Part

Verse 1:
Once more, be-fore we part,
Bless the Re-deem-er's name:
Let ev-'ry tongue and heart
Praise and a-dore the Lamb.

Refrain:
Je-sus, the sin-ner's Friend,
Him whom our souls a-dore;
His prais-es have no end:
Praise Him for-ev-er more.

Verse 2:
Lord, in Thy grace we came;
That bless-ing still im-part:
We met in Je-sus' name:
In Je-sus' name we part.

Verse 3:
Still on Thy ho-ly Word
We'd live, and feed, and grow;
Go on to know the Lord,
And prac-tice what we know.


---
Author: Robert S. Hawker (1804-1873)
Tune: SpanishHymn
