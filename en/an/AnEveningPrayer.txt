An Evening Prayer

Verse 1:
If I have wound-ed a-ny soul to-day,
If I have caused one foot to go a-stray,
If I have walked in my own will-ful way,
Dear Lord, for-give!

Verse 2:
If I have ut-tered i-dle words or vain,
If I have turned a-side from want or pain,
Lest I my-self shall suf-fer through the strain,
Dear Lord, for-give!

Verse 3:
If I have been per-verse or hard, or cold,
If I have longed for shel-ter in Thy fold,
When Thou hast giv-en me some fort to hold,
Dear Lord, for-give!

Verse 4:
For-give the sins I have con-fessed to Thee;
For-give the se-cret sins I do not see;
O guide me, love me and my keep-er be,
Dear Lord, A-men.


---
Author: C. Maude Battersby
