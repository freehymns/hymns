No Blood, No Altar Now

Verse 1:
No blood, no al-tar now,
The sac-ri-fice is o'er!
No flame, no smoke as-cends on high,
The lamb is slain no more,
But rich-er blood has flow'd from no-bler veins,
To purge the soul from guilt, and cleanse the red-dest stains.

Verse 2:
We thank Thee for the blood,
The blood of Christ, Thy Son:
The blood by which our peace is made,
Our vic-to-ry is won:
Great vic-to-ry o'er hell, and sin, and woe,
That needs no sec-ond fight, and leaves no sec-ond foe.

Verse 3:
We thank Thee for the grace,
De-scend-ing from a-bove,
That o-ver-flows out wid-est guilt,
Th'e-ter-nal Fa-ther's love.
Love of the Fa-ther's ev-er-last-ing Son,
Love of the Ho-ly Ghost, Je-ho-vah, Three in One.

Verse 4:
We thank Thee for the hope,
So glad, and sure, and clear;
It holds the droop-ing spir-it up
Till the long dawn ap-pear;
Fair hope! with what a sun-shine does it cheer
Our rough-est path on earth, our drear-iest de-sert here.

Verse 5:
We thank Thee for the crown
Of glo-ry and of life;
'Tis no poor with-'ring wreath of earth,
Man's prize in mor-tal strife;
'Tis in-cor-rupt-i-ble as is the throne,
The king-dom of our God and His in-car-nate Son.


---
Author: Hoeratius Bonar (1808-1859)
Tune: Kelso
