Not All The Blood Of Beasts

Verse 1:
Not all the blood of beasts
On Jew-ish al-tars slain
Could give the guilt-y con-science peace
Or wash a-way the stain.

Verse 2:
But Christ the heav'n-ly Lamb
Takes all our sins a-way,
A sac-ri-fice of no-bler name
And rich-er blood than they.

Verse 3:
My faith would lay its hand
On that dear head of Thine,
While, like a pen-i-tent, I stand,
And own the sin as mine.

Verse 4:
My soul looks back to see
The bur-dens Thou didst bear
When hang-ing on the curs-éd tree,
For all my guilt was there.

Verse 5:
Be-liev-ing, we re-joice
To see the curse un-done;
We bless the Lamb with cheer-ful voice
His love our hearts has won.

---
Author: Isaac Watts (1674-1748)
Tune: Boylston

---Original
