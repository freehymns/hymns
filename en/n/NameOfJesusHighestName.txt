Name Of Jesus! Highest Name

Verse 1:
Name of Je-sus! high-est Name!
Name that earth and heav'n a-dore!
From the heart of God it came,
Leads me to God's heart once more.

Verse 2:
Name of Je-sus! liv-ing tide!
Days of drought for me are past;
How much more than sat-is-fied
Are the thirst-y lips at last!

Verse 3:
Name of Je-sus! dear-est Name!
Bread of heav'n, and balm of love,
Oil of glad-ness, sur-est claim
To the treas-ures stored a-bove.

Verse 4:
On-ly Je-sus! fair-est Name!
Life and rest and peace and bliss;
Je-sus, ev-er-more the same,
He is mine and I am His!


---
Author: Gerhard Tersteegen (1697-1769)
Original Language: German
Translator: Mrs. Frances Bevan (1827-1909)
Tune: Innocents
