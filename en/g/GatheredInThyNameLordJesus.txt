Gathered In Thy Name, Lord Jesus

Verse 1:
Gath-ered in Thy name, Lord Je-sus
Lo-sing sight of all but Thee
Oh, what joy Thy pres-ence gives us,
Call-ing up our hearts to Thee!

Verse 2:
Loved with love which knows no meas-ure,
Save the Fa-ther's love to Thee,
Bless-èd Lord, our hearts would treas-ure
All the Fa-ther's thoughts of Thee.

Verse 3:
All His joy, His rest, His pleas-ure,
All His deep de-light in Thee,
Lord, Thy heart a-lone can meas-ure
What Thy Fa-ther found in Thee.

Verse 4:
How He set His love up-on Thee,
Called Thee His be-lov-èd Son;
Yet for us He did not spare Thee,
By Thy death our life was won.

Verse 5:
Oh the joy, the won-drous sing-ing,
When we see Thee as Thou art!
Thy blest name, Lord Je-sus, bring-ing
Sweet-est mu-sic to God's heart.

Verse 6:
Notes of glad-ness, songs un-ceas-ing,
Hymns of ev-er-last-ing praise,
Psalms of glo-ry, joy in-creas-ing,
Through God's end-less day of days.

Author: Miss C. A. Wellesley
