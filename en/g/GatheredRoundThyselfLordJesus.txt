Gathered 'Round Thyself, Lord Jesus

Verse 1:
Gath-ered round Thy-self, Lord Je-sus,
We now seek Thy face;
May we know Thy pres-ence with us,
Lord of grace!

Verse 2:
Love di-vine first drew us to Thee,
In our sin and need;
For our sin, in deep com-pas-sion,
Thou didst bleed.

Verse 3:
Ris-en Lord, in glo-ry seat-ed,
We are one with Thee;
Thou hast snapt the chains that bound us,
We are free.

Verse 4:
Grate-ful-ly we Thee re-mem-ber
As we break the bread,
Sym-bol of Thy bod-y giv-en
In our stead.

Verse 5:
Drink we too “the cup of bless-ing”
Which Thy love has filled;
Through Thy blood we have re-demp-tion
Fears are stilled.

Verse 6:
Back-ward look we, drawn to Cal-v’ry,
Mus-ing while we sing;
For-ward haste we to Thy com-ing,
Lord and King!
