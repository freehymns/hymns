Glory Be To Him Who Loved Us

Verse 1:
Glo-ry be to Him who loved us,
Washed us from each sin-ful stain;
Glo-ry be to Him who made us
Priests and kings with Him to reign:
Glo-ry, wor-ship, laud, and bless-ing
To the Lamb who once was slain!
Glo-ry, wor-ship, laud, and bless-ing
To the Lamb who once was slain!

Verse 2:
"Glo-ry, wor-ship, laud, and bless-ing"
Thus the choir tri-um-phant sings:
"Hon-or, rich-es, pow'r, do-min-ion,"
Thus its praise cre-a-tion brings;
Thou art wor-thy, Thou art wor-thy,
Lord of lords, and King of kings!
Thou art wor-thy, Thou art wor-thy,
Lord of lords, and King of kings!

Verse 3:
Glo-ry be to Thee, O Fa-ther,
Glo-ry be to Thee, O Son,
Glo-ry be to Thee, O Spir-it:
Glo-ry be to God a-lone,
As it was, is now, and shall be
While the end-less ag-es run!
As it was, is now, and shall be
While the end-less ag-es run!


---
Author: Horatius Bonar (1808-1889)
Tune: LookYeSaints
