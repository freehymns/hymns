Golden Deeds

Verse 1:
Each day I’ll do a gold-en deed,
By help-ing those who are in need,
My life on earth is but a span,
So I must la-bor while I can.

Refrain:
Life’s eve-ning sun is sink-ing low,
A few more days and I must go
To meet the deeds that I have done,
Where there will be no set-ting sun.

Verse 2:
God’s love a-bides on me each day,
My light must shine a-long the way;
I’ll sing His praise while ag-es roll
And strive to help some trou-bled soul.

Verse 3:
The on-ly work that will en-dure,
Is what is kind and good and pure;
And so for God I’ll take my stand,
Each day I’ll lend a help-ing hand.

Verse 4:
I’ll help some-one in time of need,
And as I do I’ll plant a seed,
Be-cause I know God's word is true,
I’ll share it with, each deed I do.

Verse 5:
And so a-long life’s wea-ry road,
My life may lift some trav-’ler’s load,
And turn some dark soul's night to day,
Make flow-ers bloom a-long the way.
