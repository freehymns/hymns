Verse 1:
God rest ye merr-y, gen-tle-men, let no-thing you dis-may,
Re-mem-ber Christ our Sav_ior was born on Christ-mas Day;

Verse 2:
In Beth-le-hem, in Ju_dah, this bless-èd Babe was born,
And laid with-in a man_ger up-on this bless-èd morn;

Now to the Lord sing prais_es all you with-in this place,
And with true love and bro-ther-hood each o-ther now em-brace;
