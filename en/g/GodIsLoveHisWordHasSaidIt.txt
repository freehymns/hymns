"God Is Love," His Word Has Said It

Verse 1:
"God is love," his word has said it,
This is news of great-est worth;
Go a-broad and quick-ly spread it,
Make it known through all the earth
That "God is love".
That "God is love".

Verse 2:
Not in yon-der bless-èd re-gions,
Where the Lord, with glo-ry crowned,
Reigns a-midst an-gel-ic le-gions,
Will the bright-est proof be found
That "God is love".
That "God is love".

Verse 3:
Tis the earth God right-ly us-es
To dis-play His bound-less love;
This the fa-vored spot He choos-es,
This the truth it teach-es of,
That "God is love".
That "God is love".

Verse 4:
He who sat en-throned in heav-en,
Came to earth where He would bleed,
And for man His life was giv-en;
This is what de-clares in-deed
That "God is love".
That "God is love".

Verse 5:
Not for those who al-ways loved him,
Did the Lord of glo-ry die,
Pit-y for the wretch-ed moved him;
Who, that hears it, will de-ny
That "God is love".
That "God is love".

Verse 6:
This great truth, go forth and spread it,
Sound the ti-dings far and near;
O may sin-ners give it cred-it,
And be joy-ful when they hear
That "God is love".
That "God is love".


---
Author: Thomas Kelly (1769-1854)
Edits: Poetic elements refined
