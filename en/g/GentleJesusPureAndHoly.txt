Gentle Jesus, Pure and Holy

Verse 1:
Gen-tle Je-sus, pure and ho-ly,
Once You were a lit-tle child,
God in hea-ven was your Fa-ther,
Ma-ry was your mo-ther mild.
Cra-dled in a man-ger low-ly,
In a sta-ble meek, for-lorn,
There be-gan the wond-rous sto-ry,
There the Prince of Peace was born!

Verse 2:
An-gels sang in heav'n a-bove You,
And there shone a bril-liant star,
And the wise men fol-lowed aft-er,
Bring-ing trea-sures from a-far;
Hap-py shep-herds joined the ma-gi,
Found the place where Je-sus lay;
And they all bowed down a-dor-ing,
Kneel-ing humb-ly in the hay.

Verse 3:
Gen-tle Je-sus, pure and ho-ly,
Lead us sin-ners un-to Thee;
May we come in true re-pent-ance
To the Child of Gal-i-lee.
Like the wise men, gifts we bring You,
Like the shep-herds, we a-dore,
Like the an-gels, songs we sing You,
Wor-ship You for-ev-er more.

---
Topic: Christmas
Tune: Beecher
Author: Charles W. Wendte (1900)
