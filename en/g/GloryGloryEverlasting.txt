Glory, Glory Everlasting

Verse 1:
Glo-ry, glo-ry ev-er-last-ing,
Be to Him who bore the cross,
Who re-deemed our souls by tast-ing,
Death, the death de-served by us!
Spread His glo-ry, Spread His glo-ry,
Who re-deemed His peo-ple thus!

Verse 2:
His is love, 'tis love un-bound-ed,
With-out meas-ure, with-out end,
Hu-man thought is here con-found-ed,
'Tis too vast to com-pre-hend!
Praise the Sav-ior! Praise the Sav-ior!
Mag-ni-fy the sin-ner's Friend!

Verse 3:
While we hear the won-drous sto-ry
Of the Sav-ior's cross and shame,
Sing we, "Ev-er-last-ing glo-ry
Be to God and to the Lamb!"
Hal-le-lu-jah! Hal-le-lu-jah!
Give ye glo-ry to His name!


---
Author: Thomas Kelly (1769-1854)
Tune: RegentSquare
