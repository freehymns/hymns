Gazing On The Lord In Glory

Verse 1:
Gaz-ing on the Lord in glo-ry,
While our hearts in wor-ship bow,
There we read the won-drous sto-ry
Of the cross, its shame and woe.

Verse 2:
Ev'-ry mark of dark dis-hon-or
Heaped up-on the thorn-crowned brow
All the depths of Thy heart's sor-row,
Told in an-sw'ring glo-ry now!

Verse 3:
On that cross, a-lone, for-sak-en,
Where no pit-ying eye was found;
Now, to God's right hand ex-alt-ed,
With Thy praise the heav'ns re-sound!

Verse 4:
Did Thy God e'en then for-sake Thee,
Hide His face from Thy deep need?
In Thy face, once marred and smit-ten,
All His glo-ry now we read.

Verse 5:
Gaz-ing on it we a-dore Thee,
Bless-èd, pre-cious, ho-ly Lord!
Thou, the Lamb, a-lone, art wor-thy -
This be earth's and heav'n's ac-cord.

Verse 6:
Rise our hearts, and bless the Fa-ther,
Cease-less song e'en here be-gun;
End-less praise and ad-o-ra-tion
To the Fa-ther and the Son!


---
Author: Miss. C. Thompson
Tune: Rhineland
Alt Tune: Dijon
