Gracious God, We Worship Thee

Verse 1:
Gra-cious God, we wor-ship Thee,
Rev-'rent-ly we bow the knee;
Je-sus Christ our on-ly plea:
Fa-ther, we a-dore Thee.

Verse 2:
Vast Thy love how deep, how wide
In the gift of Him who died;
Right-eous claims all sat-is-fied:
Fa-ther, we a-dore Thee.

Verse 3:
Low we bow be-fore Thy face,
Sons of God, O won-drous place!
Great the rich-es of Thy grace:
Fa-ther, we a-dore Thee.

Verse 4:
By Thy Spir-it grant that we
Wor-ship-pers in truth may be;
Praise, as in-cense, sweet to Thee:
Fa-ther, we a-dore Thee.

Verse 5:
Yet a-gain our song we raise,
Note of deep ador-ing praise;
Now, and soon through end-less days:
Fa-ther, we a-dore Thee.
