Sing Without Ceasing, Sing

Verse 1:
Sing with-out ceas-ing, sing
The Sav-ior's pres-ent grace.
How all things shine in light di-vine
For those who've seen His face.

Verse 2:
He's gone with-in the vail,
For us that place has won;
In Him we stand, a heav'n-ly band,
Where He Him-self is gone.

Verse 3:
There all's un-sul-lied light,
our hearts let in its rays;
And heav-'nly light makes all things bright,
Seen in that bliss-ful gaze.

Verse 4:
Such here on earth we are,
Though we in weak-ness roam;
Our place on high, God's self so nigh;
His pres-ence is our home.

Verse 5:
And stayed by joy di-vine,
As hire-lings fill the day,
Through scenes of strife and de-sert-life
We tread in peace our way.

Verse 6:
That way is up-ward still,
Where life and glo-ry are;
Our rest's a-bove; in per-fect love
The glo-ry we shall share.

Verse 7:
For-ev-er with the Lord,
For-ev-er like Him then,
And see His face in that blest place,
Our Fa-ther's house in heav'n.


---
Author: John Nelson Darby (1800-1882)
Tune: StThomas
