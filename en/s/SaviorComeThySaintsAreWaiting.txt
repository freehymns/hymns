Savior, Come, Thy Saints Are Waiting

Verse 1:
Sav-ior, come, Thy saints are wait-ing,
Wait-ing for the ad-vent day,
For their time of lib-er-at-ing;
Come, and bear Thy saints a-way.
Come, Lord Je-sus, come, Lord Je-sus,
Thus Thy wait-ing peo-ple pray.

Verse 2:
Base and mean the vain en-dea-vor,
While on earth to find our rest;
Till we see Thy face, we nev-er
Shall or can be ful-ly blest;
In thy pres-ence, in thy presence
No-thing shall our peace mol-est.

Verse 3:
Lord, we wait for Thine ap-pear-ing;
"Tar-ry not," Thy peo-ple say;
Bright the pros-pect is, and cheer-ing,
Of be-hold-ing Thee that day;
When our sor-row, when our sor-row
Shall for-ev-er pass a-way.

Verse 4:
Till it comes, O keep us stead-y!
Keep us walk-ing in Thy ways;
At Thy call may we be read-y,
On Thee, Lord, with joy to gaze;
And in heav-en, and in heav-en
Sing Thine ev-er-last-ing praise.


---
Author: Thomas Kelly (1769-1854)
Tune: RegentSquare
