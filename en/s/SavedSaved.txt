Saved, Saved

Verse 1:
I’ve found a friend, who is all to me,
His love is ev-er true;
I love to tell how He lift-ed me
And what His grace can do for you.

Refrain:
Saved by His pow'r di-vine,
Saved to new life sub-lime!
Life now is sweet and my joy is com-plete,
For I’m saved, saved, saved!

Verse 2:
He saves me from ev-'ry sin and harm,
Se-cures my soul each day;
I’m lean-ing strong on His might-y arm;
I know He’ll guide me all the way.

Verse 3:
When poor and need-y and all a-lone,
In love He said to me,
"Come un-to Me and I’ll lead you home,
To live with Me e-ter-nal-ly."


---
Author: John Prentice Scholfield, 1911
Tune: Rapture
