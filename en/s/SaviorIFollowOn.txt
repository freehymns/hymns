Savior, I Follow On

Verse 1:
Sav-ior, I fol-low on,
Guid-ed by Thee,
See-ing not yet the hand
That lead-eth me.
Hushed be my heart and still;
Fear I no fur-ther ill.
On-ly to meet Thy will
My will shall be.

Verse 2:
Riv-en the rock for me,
Thirst to re-lieve,
Man-na from heav-en falls
Fresh ev-'ry eve.
Nev-er a want se-vere
Caus-eth my eye a tear,
But Thou dost whis-per near,
"On-ly be-lieve."

Verse 3:
Of-ten to Mar-ah's brink
Have I been brought;
Shrink-ing the cup to drink,
Help I have sought,
And with the prayer's as-cent
Je-sus the branch hath rent,
Quick-ly re-lief hath sent,
Sweet-'ning the draught.

Verse 4:
Sav-ior, I long to walk
Clos-er with Thee;
Led by Thy guid-ing hand,
Ev-er to be
Con-stant-ly near Thy side,
Quick-ened and pu-ri-fied,
Liv-ing for Him who died
Free-ly for me.


---
Author: Robinson, Charles S. (1829-1899)
Tune: StEdmund
