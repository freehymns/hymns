Savior Of Sinners

Verse 1:
Christ is the Sav-ior of sin-ners,
Christ is the Sav-ior for me;
Long I was chained in sin’s dark-ness,
Now by His grace I am free.

Refrain:
Sav-ior of sin-ners,
Sav-ior of sin-ners like me,
Shed-ding His blood for my ran-som,
Christ is the Sav-ior for me.

Verse 2:
Now I can say I am par-doned,
Hap-py and jus-ti-fied, free,
Saved by my bless-èd Re-deem-er,
This is the Sav-ior for me. [Cho-rus]

Verse 3:
Just as I was He re-ceived me,
Seek-ing from judg-ment to flee,
Now there is no con-dem-na-tion,
This is the Sav-ior for me. [Cho-rus]

Verse 4:
Soon shall the glo-ry be dawn-ing,
Then His own face I shall see,
Sing, O my soul, in thy glad-ness,
This is the Sav-ior for me. [Cho-rus]


---
Author: Heyman Wreford
Tune: ShowersOfBlessing
