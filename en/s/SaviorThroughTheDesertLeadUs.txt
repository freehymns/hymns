Savior, Through The Desert Lead Us

Verse 1:
Sav-ior, through the de-sert lead us,
With-out Thee we can-not go;
Thou from cru-el chains hast freed us,
Thou hast laid the tyr-ant low:
Let Thy pres-ence, Let Thy pres-ence,
Cheer us all our jour-ney through.
Let Thy pres-ence, Let Thy pres-ence,
Cheer us all our jour-ney through.

Verse 2:
With a price Thy love has bought us:
(Sav-ior, what a love is thine!)
Hith-er-to Thy pow'r has brought us;
(pow'r and love in Thee com-bine;)
Lord of glo-ry, Lord of glo-ry,
Ev-er on thine Is-rael shine.
Lord of glo-ry, Lord of glo-ry,
Ev-er on thine Is-rael shine.

Verse 3:
Through a de-sert waste and cheer-less,
Though our des-tined jour-ney lie,
Rend-ered by Thy pres-ence fear-less,
We may ev-'ry foe de-fy;
Nought shall move us, Nought shall move us,
While we see our Sav-ior nigh.
Nought shall move us, Nought shall move us,
While we see our Sav-ior nigh.


---
Author: Thomas Kelly (1769-1854)
Tune: Bradbury
