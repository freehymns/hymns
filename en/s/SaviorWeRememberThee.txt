Savior, We Remember Thee!

Verse 1:
Sav-ior, we re-mem-ber Thee!
Thy deep woe and ag-o-ny,
All Thy suf-f’ring on the tree,
Sav-ior, we a-dore Thee!

Verse 2:
Cal-va-ry! O Cal-va-ry!
Mer-cy’s vast un-fath-omed sea
Love, e-ter-nal love to me.
Sav-ior, we a-dore Thee!

Verse 3:
Dark-ness hung a-round Thy head,
When for sin Thy blood was shed,
Vic-tim in the sin-ner’s stead.
Sav-ior, we a-dore Thee!

Verse 4:
Je-sus, lo, Thou now art ris’n!
Thou hast all our sins for-giv’n;
Haste we to our home in heav’n.
Sav-ior, we a-dore Thee!

Verse 5:
Soon, with joy-ful, glad sur-prise,
We shall hear Thy word, "A-rise!"
Mount-ing up-ward to the skies.
Glo-ry, glo-ry, glo-ry!

Verse 6:
Sav-ior, we Thy love a-dore;
We will praise Thee more and more;
Spread Thy name from shore to shore!
Sav-ior, we a-dore Thee!


---
Author: S. Trevor Francis, 1835
Tune: Zacher
