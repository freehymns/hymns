Satisfied With Thee, Lord Jesus

Verse 1:
Sat-is-fied with Thee, Lord Je-sus,
I am blest;
Peace which pass-eth un-der-stand-ing,
On Thy breast;
No more doubt-ing, no more trem-bling,
Oh, what rest! Oh, what rest!

Verse 2:
Oc-cu-pied with Thee, Lord Je-sus,
In Thy grace;
All Thy ways and thoughts a-bout me
On-ly trace
Deep-er sto-ries of the glo-ries
Of Thy grace, of Thy grace.

Verse 3:
Tak-en up with Thee, Lord Je-sus,
I would be;
Find-ing joy and sat-is-fac-tion
All in Thee;
Thou the near-est and the dear-est
Un-to me, un-to me.

Verse 4:
Lis-ten-ing for Thy shout, Lord Je-sus,
In the air!
When Thy saints shall rise with joy to
Meet Thee there;
O what glad-ness! no more sad-ness,
Sin nor care, sin nor care.


---
Author: Henry Bennett (1813–1868)
Tune: Satisfied
