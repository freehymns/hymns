Sweet The Moments

Verse 1:
Sweet the mo-ments which, in bless-ing,
Mus-ing o'er the cross we spend;
Life and health and peace pos-sess-ing
From the sin-ners' dy-ing Friend.

Verse 2:
Here we rest, in won-der view-ing
All our guilt on Je-sus laid!
And a full re-demp-tion flow-ing
From the sac-ri-fice He made.

Verse 3:
Here we find the dawn of heav-en,
While up-on the Lamb we gaze,
See our tres-pass-es for-giv-en,
And our songs of tri-umph raise.

Verse 4:
Oh, that strong in faith a-bid-ing,
We may to the Sav-ior cleave,
Naught with Him our hearts di-vid-ing,
All for Him con-tent to leave!

Verse 5:
May we still, God's mind dis-cern-ing,
To the Lamb for wis-dom go:
There new won-ders dai-ly learn-ing,
All the depths of mer-cy know.


---
Author: James Allen, 1787
Revisions: Walter Shirley (1725-1786)
Tune: Mariners
