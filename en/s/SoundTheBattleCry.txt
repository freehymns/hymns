Sound The Battle Cry!

Verse 1:
Sound the bat-tle cry! See, the foe is nigh;
Raise the stand-ard high for the Lord;
Gird your ar-mor on, stand firm ev-'ry one;
Rest your cause up-on His ho-ly Word.

Refrain:
Rouse, then, sol-diers, ral-ly round the ban-ner,
Read-y, stead-y, pass the word a-long;
On-ward, for-ward, shout a-loud Ho-san-na!
Christ is cap-tain of the might-y throng.

Verse 2:
Strong to meet the foe, march-ing on we go,
While our cause we know, must pre-vail;
Shield and ban-ner bright, gleam-ing in the light,
Batt-ling for the right we ne’er can fail.

Verse 3:
O! Thou God of all, hear us when we call,
Help us one and all by Thy grace;
When the bat-tle’s done, and the vic-t’ry’s won,
May we wear the crown be-fore Thy face.


---
Author: William Fiske Sherwin, 1869
