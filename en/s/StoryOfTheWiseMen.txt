Story of the Wise Men

Verse 1:
Long a-go the glor-ious Christ-mas star
Guid-ed wise men from a-far,
To the lit-tle town of Beth-le-hem,
To the Sav-ior of all men.

Refrain:
May we like those wise men.
Seek Him. Hum-bly kneel. Kneel a-gain.

Verse 2:
O-ver hill, a-cross the de-sert sand,
Slow-ly t’ward the Ho-ly Land,
With them pre-cious lov-ing gifts they bring,
Ask-ing for the new-born King.

Verse 3:
When they saw the Child they all re-joiced,
Wor-shipped Him with ten-der voice,
Bow-ing low, pre-sent-ed gifts of love
To the Christ-child from a-bove.

---
Topic: Christmas
Author: Ida F. Leyda (1916)
Author, refrain: Chuck Morris (2025)
