Sunrise

Verse 1:
When I shall come to the end of my way,
When I shall rest at the close of life’s day,
When Wel-come home I shall hear Je-sus say,
O that will be sun-rise for me.

Refrain:
Sun-rise to-mor-row, sun-rise to-mor-row,
Sun-rise in glo-ry is wait-ing for me;
Sun-rise to-mor-row, sun-rise to-mor-row,
Sun-rise with Je-sus for e-ter-ni-ty.

Verse 2:
When in His beau-ty I see the great King,
Join with the ran-som-ed His praises to sing,
When I shall join them my trib-utes to bring,
O that will be sun-rise for me.

Verse 3:
When life is o-ver and day-light is passed,
In Heav-en’s har-bor my an-chor is cast,
When I see Je-sus my Sav-ior at last,
O that will be sun-rise for me.


---
Author: William C. Poole, 1924
