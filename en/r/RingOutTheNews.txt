Ring Out the News

Verse 1:
Ring out the news that Christ is born,
Come, Christ-ians, tell the sto-ry
That was pro-claimed on Christ-mas morn,
By an-gel choirs from glo-ry.

Refrain (sing twice):
Ring peace on earth, sweet peace
On earth, good will to men;
Ring peace on earth and glo-ry,
Glo-ry in the high-est.

Verse 2:
Tell o'er a-gain the Sav-ior’s birth,
The joy-ful sound keep ring-ing;
Tell how for us He came to earth,
While shep-herds heard sweet sing-ing.

Versee 3:
The shep-herds went to Beth-le-hem
And found the lit-tle Stran-ger
Just as the an-gels said to them,
And ly-ing in a man-ger.

Verse 4:
For us He came to earth to die,
That we might be for-giv-en;
He came that we at last on high
His praise might sing in Hea-ven.
