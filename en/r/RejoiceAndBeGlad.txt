Rejoice And Be Glad

Verse 1:
Re-joice and be glad!
The Re-deem-er has come!
Go_ look on His cra-dle, His cross, and His tomb.

Refrain:
Sound His prais-es, tell the sto-ry,

Re-joice and be glad!
It is sun-shine at last!
The_ clouds have de-part-ed, the shad-ows are past.

Verse 3:
Re-joice and be glad!
For the blood hath been shed;
Re_demp-tion is fin-ished, the price has been paid.

Verse 4:
Re-joice and be glad!
Now the par-don is free!
The_ Just for the un-just has died on the tree.

Verse 5:
Re-joice and be glad!
For the Lamb that was slain
O’er_ death is tri-um-phant, and liv-eth a-gain.

Verse 6:
Re-joice and be glad!
For our king is on high,
He_ plead-eth for us on His throne in the sky.

Verse 7:
Re-joice and be glad!
For He com-eth a-gain;
He_ com-eth in glo-ry, the Lamb that was slain.
