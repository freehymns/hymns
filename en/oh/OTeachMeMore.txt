O Teach Me More Of Thy Blest Ways

Verse 1:
O teach me more of Thy blest ways!
Thou ho-ly Lamb of God,
And fix and root me in Thy grace,
As one re-deemed by blood.

Verse 2:
O tell me of-ten of Thy love,
Of all Thy grief and pain!
And let my heart with joy con-fess
That thence comes all my gain.

Verse 3:
For this, O may I free-ly count
What-e’er I have but loss,
The dear-est ob-ject of my love,
Com-pared with Thee, but dross.

Verse 4:
En-grave this deep-ly on my heart
With an e-ter-nal pen,
That I may, in some small de-gree,
Re-turn Thy love a-gain.


---
Author: James Hutton (1715-1795)
Tune: Sawley
