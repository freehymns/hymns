O Thou, Whose Bounty Fills Our Cup

Verse 1:
O Thou, whose boun-ty fills our cup
With ev-'ry bless-ing meet!
We give Thee thanks for ev-'ry drop,
The bit-ter and the sweet.

Verse 2:
We thank Thee for the de-sert road,
And for the riv-er side;
For all Thy good-ness has be-stowed,
And all Thy grace de-nied.

Verse 3:
We thank Thee for the smile and frown,
And for the gain and loss;
We bless Thee for the fu-ture crown,
And for the pres-ent cross.

Verse 4:
We bless Thee for the glad in-crease,
And for the wan-ing joy;
And for the calm and set-tled peace
Which noth-ing can de-stroy.


---
Author: Mrs. Jane Crewdson (1809-1863)
Tune: Arlington
