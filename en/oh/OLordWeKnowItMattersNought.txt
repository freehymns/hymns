O Lord, We Know It Matters Nought

Verse 1:
O Lord, we know it mat-ters nought,
How sweet the song may be,
No heart but of the Spir-it taught
Makes mel-o-dy to Thee.

Verse 2:
Then teach Thy gath-ered saints, O Lord,
To wor-ship in Thy fear;
And let Thy grace mould ev-'ry word
That meets Thy ho-ly ear.

Verse 3:
Thou hast by blood made sin-ners meet,
As saints in light, to come
And wor-ship at the mer-cy-seat,
Be-fore th'e-ter-nal throne.

Verse 4:
Thy pre-cious name is all we show,
Our on-ly pass-port, Lord;
And full as-sur-ance now we know,
Con-fid-ing in Thy word.

Verse 5:
O large-ly give, 'tis all Thine own,
The Spir-it's good-ly fruit;
Praise, flow-ing forth in life, a-lone
Our liv-ing Lord can suit.


---
Author: Mary Bowley Peters (1813-1856)
Tune: Martyrdom
