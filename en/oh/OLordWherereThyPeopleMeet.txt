O Lord, Where're Thy People Meet

Verse 1:
O Lord, wher-e'er Thy peo-ple meet,
There they be-hold Thy mer-cy seat:
Wher-e'er they seek Thee, Thou art found,
And ev-'ry place is hal-lowed ground.

Verse 2:
Here may we prove the pow'r of prayer
To strength-en faith and ban-ish care;
To teach our faint de-sires to rise,
And bring all heav'n be-fore our eyes.

Verse 3:
Lord! We are weak, but Thou art near,
Nor short Thine arm, nor deaf Thine ear;
Oh, fill us with Thy grace di-vine,
And may our hearts be whol-ly Thine!
