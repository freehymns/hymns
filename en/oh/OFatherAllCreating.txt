O Father, All Creating

Verse 1:
O Fa-ther, all cre-at-ing,
Whose wis-dom, love, and pow'r
First bound two lives to-geth-er
In E-den’s pri-mal hour,
To-day to these Thy chil-dren
Thine ear-liest gifts re-new,
A home by Thee made hap-py,
A love by Thee kept true.

Verse 2:
O Sav-ior, Guest most boun-teous
Of old in Gal-i-lee,
Vouch-safe to-day Thy pres-ence
With these who call on Thee;
Their store of earth-ly glad-ness
Trans-form to heav'n-ly wine,
And teach them, in the tast-ing,
To know the gift is Thine.

Verse 3:
O Spir-it of the Fa-ther,
Breathe on them from a-bove,
So might-y in Thy pure-ness,
So tend-er in Thy love;
That, guard-ed by Thy pres-ence,
From sin and strife kept free,
Their lives may own Thy guid-ance,
Their hearts be ruled by Thee.

Verse 4:
Ex-cept Thou build it, Fa-ther,
The house is built in vain;
Ex-cept Thou, Sav-ior, bless it,
The joy will turn to pain;
But naught can break the un-ion
Of hearts in Thee made one;
And love Thy Spir-it hal-lows
Is end-less love be-gun.


---
Author: John Ellerton, 1876
Tune: DayOfRest
Topic: Marriage
