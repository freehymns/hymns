Oh Scenes Of Heavenly Joy

Verse 1:
Oh scenes of heav'n-ly joy!
The Fa-ther's house a bove,
Where cloud-less peace with out al-loy,
Fills all that home of love.

Verse 2:
There glo-ry bright and fair,
Shines with ce-les-tial beam;
For He who suf-fered once is there,
Its cen-ter and its theme.

Verse 3:
The Fa-ther's full de-light
Is cen-tered in the Son,
And count-less tongues in heav'n u-nite
To tell what He hath done.

Verse 4:
The Lamb en-throned shall there
En-gage each rap-tured heart,
While myr-iad saints Christ's like-ness bear
And have with Him their part.

Verse 5:
'Tis He who made us meet
With saints in light to dwell ;
And now we chant His prais-es sweet,
Whose love we know so well.

Verse 6:
E'en now we taste the love,
And know the might-y pow'r,
By which we'll rise to realms a-bove
When wait-ing time is o'er.

Verse 7:
Praise Him a-gain, a-gain —
For us the cross He bore ;
Now all is Yea, and all A-men,
In Him for ev-er-more.


---
Author: T. Porter, 1900
Tune: Dennis
