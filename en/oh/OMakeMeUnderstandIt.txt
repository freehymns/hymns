O Make Me Understand It

Verse 1:
Give me a sight, O Sav-ior!
Of Thy won-drous love to me,
Of the love that brought Thee down to earth
To die on Cal-va-ry.

Refrain:
O make me un-der-stand it!
Help me to take it in!
What it meant to Thee, the Ho-ly One,
To bear a-way my sin.

Verse 2:
Was it the nails, O Sav-ior!
That bound Thee to the tree?
Nay, ’twas Thine ev-er-last-ing love,
Thy love for me, for me.

Verse 3:
O won-der of all won-ders!
That through Thy death for me
My o-pen sins, my se-cret sins,
Can all for-giv-en be.

Verse 4:
Then melt my heart, O Sav-ior!
Bend me, yea, break me down
Un-til I own Thee Con-quer-or,
And Lord and Sov-er-eign crown.


---
Author: Katherine Agnes May Kelly (1869–1942)
Tune: OMakeMeUnderstandIt
