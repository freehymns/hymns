Praise The Lord, Who Died To Save Us

Verse 1:
Praise the Lord, who died to save us,
Praise His ev-er gra-cious name;
Praise Him that He lives to bless us,
Now and ev-er-more the same:
Bless-ed Sav-ior! Bless-ed Sav-ior!
We would all Thy love pro-claim.

Verse 2:
Grace it was, yea, grace a-bound-ing,
Brought Thee down to save the lost;
Ye a-bove, His throne sur-round-ing,
Praise Him, praise Him all His host;
Saints! A-dore Him! Saints! A-dore Him!
Ye are they who owe Him most.

Verse 3:
We, of all His hand cre-at-ed,
Ob-jects of such grace a-lone,
By e-ter-nal love e-lect-ed,
Des-tined now to share His throne,
Sing with won-der, Sing with won-der,
Sing of what our Lord hath done.

Verse 4:
Praise His Name, who died to save us;
'Tis by Him His peo-ple live;
And in Him the Fa-ther gave us
All that bound-less love could give:
Life e-ter-nal, Life e-ter-nal
In our Sav-ior we re-ceive.


---
Author: Thomas Kelly
Tune: Dismissal
