Praise Ye The Lord, Again, Again.

Verse 1:
"Praise ye the Lord," a-gain, a-gain.
The Spir-it strik-es the chord;
Nor touch-eth He our hearts in vain;
We praise, we praise the Lord.

Verse 2:
"Re-joice in Him," a-gain, a-gain,
The Spir-it speaks the word,
And faith tak-es up the hap-py strain;
Our joy is in in the Lord.

Verse 3:
"Stand fast in Christ," ah, yet a-gain
He tea-cheth all the band!
Since hu-man ef-forts are in vain,
In Christ it is we stand.

Verse 4:
"Clean ev-'ry whit;" Thou saidst it, Lord;
Shall one sus-pi-cion lurk?
Thine, sure-ly, is a faith-ful word,
And Thine a fin-ished work.

Verse 5:
For-ev-er be the glo-ry giv'n
To Thee, O Lamb of God!
Our ev-'ry joy on earth, in heav'n,
We owe it to Thy blood.


---
Author: Mary Bowley Peters (1813-1856)
Tune: Arlington
