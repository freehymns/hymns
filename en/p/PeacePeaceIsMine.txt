Peace, Peace Is Mine

Verse 1:
God's al-might-y arms are round me;
Peace, peace is mine!
Judg-ment scenes need not to con-found me;
Peace, peace is mine!
Je-sus came Him-self and sought me:
Sold to death, He found and bought me;
Then my bless-ed free-dom taught me,
Peace, peace is mine!

Verse 2:
While I hear life's rug-ged bil-lows,
Peace, peace is mine!
Why sus-pend my harp on wil-lows?
Peace, peace is mine!
I may sing, with Christ be-side me,
Though a thou-sand ills be-tide me;
Safe-ly He hath sworn to guide me:
Peace, peace is mine!

Verse 3:
Ev-'ry tri-al draws Him near-er;
Peace, peace is mine!
All His strokes but make Him dear-er;
Peace, peace is mine!
Bless I, then, the hand that smit-eth
Gen-tly, and to heal de-light-eth;
'Tis a-gainst my sins He fight-eth:
Peace, peace is mine!

Verse 4:
Wel-come, ev-'ry ris-ing sun-light,
Peace, peace is mine!
Near-er home each roll-ing mid-night:
Peace, peace is mine!
Death and hell can-not ap-pall me,
Safe in Christ what-ev'r be-fall me;
Calm-ly wait I till He call me:
Peace, peace is mine!


---
Author: J. Denham Smith (1817-1889)
Tune: Evensong
