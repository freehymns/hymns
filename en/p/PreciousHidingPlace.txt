Precious Hiding Place

Verse 1:
I was stray-ing when Christ found me
In the night so dark and cold;
Ten-der-ly His arm went round me,
And He bore me to His fold.

Refrain:
Pre-cious hid-ing place,
Pre-cious hid-ing place,
In the shel-ter of His love;
Not a doubt or fear,
Since my Lord is near,
And I’m shel-tered in His love.

Verse 2:
With His nail-scarred hand He brought me
To the shel-ter of His love;
Of His grace and will He taught me,
And of heav’n-ly rest a-bove.

Verse 3:
Though the night be dark a-round me,
I am safe, for He is near;
Nev-er shall my foes con-found me,
While the Sav-ior’s voice I hear.


---
Author: Avis M. Christiansen, 1918
