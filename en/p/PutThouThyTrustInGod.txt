Put Thou Thy Trust in God

Verse 1:
Put thou thy trust in God,
In du-ty's path go on;
Walk in His strength with faith and hope,
So shall thy work be done.

Verse 2:
Com-mit thy ways to Him,
Thy works in-to His hands,
And rest on His un-chang-ing word,
Who heav-en and earth com-mand-s.

Verse 3:
Though years on years roll on,
His cov-e-nant shall en-dure;
Though clouds and dark-ness hide His path,
The prom-ised grace is sure.

Verse 4:
Through waves, and clouds, and storms
His pow-er will clear the way;
Wait thou His time, the dark-est night
Shall end in bright-est day.

Verse 5:
Leave to His sov'-reign sway
To choose and to com-mand;
So shalt thou, wond'-ring, own His way
How wise, how strong His hand.
