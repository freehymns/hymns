Praise His Name Forever

Verse 1:
Glo-ry, hon-or, maj-es-ty and pow-er
Be to Him now ris-en from the dead,
Who from death hath tak-en ev'r-y ter-ror,
And hath made the grave a vic-tor's bed.

Refrain:
Praise His name for-ev-er,
[Be sure to] Laud and hon-or
Christ our ris-en king!
Migh-ty to de-liv-er,
[With an-gels] We His praise will sing.

Refrain:@e1
Praise His name for-ev-er, praise his ho-ly name,
Laud and praise and hon-or
Christ our ris-en king!
Migh-ty to de-liv-er, merc-i-ful to save,
With the an-gels we His praise will sing.

Verse 2:
Songs of joy we sing in ex-ul-ta-tion,
Of our king, Re-deem-er, Lord of all!
For He lives, o'er sin and death vic-tor-ious;
Earth and Heav'n His might-y deeds ex-tol.

Verse 3:
An-gels praise Him, in the high-est glo-ry,
Cher-u-bim and ser-a-phim a-dore;
While our hu-man hearts a-wake the cho-rus,
Je-sus lives! He lives for-ev-er-more!
