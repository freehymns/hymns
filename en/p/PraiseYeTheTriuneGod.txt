Praise Ye The Triune God

Verse 1:
Praise ye the Fa-ther for His lov-ing-kind-ness;
Ten-der-ly cares He for His err-ing chil-dren;
Praise Him, ye an-gels, praise Him in the heav-ens,
Praise ye Je-ho_vah!

Verse 2:
Praise ye the Sav-ior; Great is His com-pas-sion;
Gra-cious-ly cares He for His cho-sen peo-ple;
Young men and maid-ens, old-er folks and chil-dren,
Praise ye the Sav_ior!

Verse 3:
Praise ye the Spir-it, Com-fort-er and keep-er,
Sent of the Fa-ther and the Son to bless us;
Praise ye the Fa-ther, Son and Ho-ly Spir-it,
Praise ye the Tri-une God!


---
Author:  Elizabeth R. Charles, circa 1858
Tune: Flemming
Edits: Revised verse 3 to avoid Replacement Theology controversy.
