Everlasting Glory Be

Verse 1:
Ev-er-last-ing glo-ry be,
God and Fa-ther, un-to Thee
'Tis with joy Thy chil-dren raise
Hearts and voic-es in Thy praise.
Hearts and voic-es in Thy praise.

Verse 2:
Thine the light that showed our sin,
Showed how guilt-y we had been:
Thine the love that free-ly gave
Thine own Son our souls to save.
Thine own Son our souls to save.

Verse 3:
Called to share the rest of God
In the Fa-ther's blest a-bode,
God of love and God of light,
In Thy prais-es we u-nite.
In Thy prais-es we u-nite.

Verse 4:
Glad-ly we Thy grace pro-claim,
Know-ing now the Fa-ther's name:
God and Fa-ther, un-to Thee
Ev-er-last-ing glo-ry be!
Ev-er-last-ing glo-ry be!


---
Author: J. Wilson Smith
Tune: Hendon
