Endless Praises To The Lord

Verse 1:
End-less prais-es to our Lord!
Ev-er be His name a-dored!
An-gels crown Him, crown the Lamb:
He is wor-thy, praise His name.
He is wor-thy, praise His name.

Verse 2:
Saints a-dore Him, sound His fame,
You He saves from end-less shame;
Saints and an-gels, joint-ly sing:
Glo-ry, glo-ry to our King!
Glo-ry, glo-ry to our King!

---
Author: Thomas Kelly (1769-1854)
Source: "Public, Parlour, and Cottage Hymns. A New Selection" John J. Harrod, Baltimore, 1823.  Part II, Hymn 74.  https://hymnary.org/hymn/PPCH1823/page/240
Tune: Hendon
