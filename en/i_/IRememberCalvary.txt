I Remember Calvary

Verse 1:
Where He may lead me I will go,
For I have learned to trust Him so,
And I re-mem-ber ’twas for me,
That He was slain on Cal-va-ry.

Refrain:
Je-sus shall lead me night and day,
Je-sus shall lead me all the way,
He is the tru-est friend to me,
For I re-mem-ber Cal-va-ry.

Verse 2:
O I de-light in His com-mand,
Love to be led by His dear hand;
His di-vine will is sweet to me,
Hal-lowed by blood-stained Cal-va-ry.

Verse 3:
On-ward I go, nor doubt nor fear,
Hap-py with Christ my Sav-ior near,
Trust-ing that I some day shall see,
Je-sus my friend of Cal-va-ry.
