I Need Thee Ev'ry Hour

Verse 1:
I need Thee ev-'ry hour,
Most gra-cious Lord;
No tend-er voice like Thine
Can peace af-ford.

Refrain:
I need Thee, O I need Thee;
Ev-'ry hour I need Thee;
O bless me now, my Sav-ior,
I come to Thee.

Verse 2:
I need Thee ev-'ry hour,
Stay Thou near-by;
Temp-ta-tions lose their pow'r
When Thou art nigh.

Verse 3:
I need Thee ev-'ry hour,
In joy or pain;
Come quick-ly and a-bide,
Or life is vain.

Verse 4:
I need Thee ev-'ry hour;
Teach me Thy will;
And Thy rich prom-is-es
In me ful-fill.

Verse 5:
I need Thee ev-'ry hour,
Most Ho-ly One;
O make me Thine in-deed,
Thou bless-èd Son.


---
Author: Annie S. Hawks, 1871
Author, Refrain: Robert Lowry (1826–1899)
