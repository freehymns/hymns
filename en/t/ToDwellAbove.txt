To Dwell Above

Verse 1:
What will it be to dwell a-bove,
And with the Lord of glo-ry reign,
Since the blest knowl-edge of His love
So bright-ens all this drear-y plain?
No heart can think, no tongue can tell,
What joy 'twill be with Christ to dwell!
No heart can think, no tongue can tell,
What joy 'twill be with Christ to dwell!

Verse 2:
When sin no more ob-structs the sight,
And flesh and sense de-ceive no more,
When we shall see the Prince of life,
And all His works of grace ex-plore:
What heights and depths of love di-vine,
Will there through end-less ag-es shine!
What heights and depths of love di-vine,
Will there through end-less ag-es shine!

Verse 3:
And God has fixed the hap-py day,
When the last tear shall dim our eyes,
When He will wipe these tears a-way,
And fill our hearts with glad sur-prise;
To hear His voice, and see His face,
And know the ful-ness of His grace.
To hear His voice, and see His face,
And know the ful-ness of His grace.

Author: Joseph Swain (1761-1796)
