"This Do, Remember Me"

Verse 1:
"This do, re-mem-ber Me";
O bless-èd, liv-ing Lord,
What depths of grace we now can trace
In that most pre-cious word!

Verse 2:
"This do, re-mem-ber Me";
Oh, deep de-sire of love,
As 'round Thy soul those waves did roll,
God's judg-ment from a-bove.

Verse 3:
"This do, re-mem-ber Me";
Oh, what a sa-vor sweet;
For God a-bove! for man, what love!
In all Thy work com-plete!

Verse 4:
Now gath-ered round Thy-self,
With heart and con-science free,
O Lord, once dead, our liv-ing Head,
We do re-mem-ber Thee!


---
Author: James G. Deck (1807-1884)
Tune: Boylston
