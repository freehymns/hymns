Tell It to Jesus

Verse 1:
Are you wea-ry, are you heav-y heart-ed?
Tell it to Je-sus, tell it to Je-sus.
Are you griev-ing o-ver joys de-part-ed?
Tell it to Je-sus a-lone.

Refrain:
Tell it to Je-sus, tell it to Je-sus,
He is a friend that’s well known.
You’ve no oth-er such a friend or broth-er,
Tell it to Je-sus a-lone.

Verse 2:
Do the tears flow down your cheeks un-bid-den?
Tell it to Je-sus, tell it to Je-sus.
Have you sins that to men’s eyes are hid-den?
Tell it to Je-sus a-lone.

Verse 3:
Do you fear the gath-'ring clouds of sor-row?
Tell it to Je-sus, tell it to Je-sus.
Are you anx-ious what shall be to-mor-row?
Tell it to Je-sus a-lone.

Verse 4:
Are you trou-bled at the thought of dy-ing?
Tell it to Je-sus, tell it to Je-sus.
For Christ’s com-ing king-dom are you sigh-ing?
Tell it to Je-sus a-lone.


---
Author: Edmund Simon Lorenz (1854–1942)
Original Language: German
Translator: Jeremiah Rankin (1828–1904)
